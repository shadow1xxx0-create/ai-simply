Mubert generates original, royalty-free AI music for videos, podcasts, streams, and apps. Unlike libraries, every track is generated uniquely based on mood, activity, and length — so your content always has a perfectly fitting original soundtrack. It's built for content creators who need background music without copyright strikes.

## Key Features

- AI-generated music by mood, genre, activity, and duration
- Royalty-free for commercial use
- Personal playlist generation via Mubert Play
- API for developers to embed music generation
- Render custom-length tracks (30s to 25 minutes)
- Integration with creative tools

## Pricing

- **Free**: 25 tracks/month, personal use only
- **Creator ($14/month)**: 500 tracks/month, commercial license
- **Pro ($39/month)**: Unlimited tracks, attribution-free
- **Business ($199/month)**: API access, team seats

## Best For

YouTubers, podcasters, streamers, and app developers who need unique, copyright-safe background music generated on demand.
