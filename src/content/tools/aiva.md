AIVA is an AI music composition tool designed for professional creators who need original soundtracks. It generates full orchestral, cinematic, and electronic compositions in dozens of styles — and on paid plans, you own full copyright to everything you create. Used by film composers, game developers, and advertising agencies.

## Key Features

- AI composition in 250+ musical styles
- Full orchestral, cinematic, and electronic genres
- Edit and influence compositions with parameters
- Download in MP3, WAV, and MIDI formats
- Copyright ownership on paid plans
- Influence tracks using existing songs as reference

## Pricing

- **Free**: 3 downloads/month, AIVA retains copyright
- **Standard ($15/month)**: 15 downloads/month, monetization rights
- **Pro ($49/month)**: Unlimited downloads, full copyright

## Best For

Film composers, game developers, and advertising creatives who need original, professional-quality music with full copyright ownership — without hiring a composer or paying licensing fees.
