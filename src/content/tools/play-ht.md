Play.ht offers ultra-realistic AI text-to-speech with one of the largest voice libraries available — 900+ voices across 142 languages. It powers podcasts, audiobooks, e-learning courses, and voiceovers with voices indistinguishable from real humans. Its PlayDialog model produces conversational, expressive speech that sounds genuinely natural.

## Key Features

- 900+ AI voices in 142 languages
- Ultra-realistic conversational speech (PlayDialog)
- Voice cloning from audio samples
- Audio blog and article-to-podcast conversion
- WordPress plugin for auto-audio
- API for developers
