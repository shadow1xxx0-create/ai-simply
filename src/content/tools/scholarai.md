ScholarAI gives you instant access to 200+ million peer-reviewed papers through a conversational AI interface. Ask any research question and it searches real academic databases, summarizes findings, and cites exact sources — so you can trust every answer. Available as a ChatGPT plugin and standalone web app.

## Key Features

- Search 200M+ peer-reviewed papers
- AI summaries with exact citations
- Full-text access to open-access papers
- ChatGPT and Claude plugin integration
- Literature review generation
- Save and organize research sessions

## Pricing

- **Free**: 10 searches/month
- **Pro ($9.99/month)**: Unlimited searches, full-text access

## Best For

Researchers, students, and professionals who need fast, cited answers from peer-reviewed literature — not hallucinated facts from a general AI.
