Fathom is a free AI notetaker that sits in your Zoom calls and generates instant summaries the moment the meeting ends. No more manual notes — Fathom highlights key moments, action items, and decisions so you can stay focused on the conversation.

## Key Features
- Free unlimited recording and transcription
- Instant AI summary post-call
- Highlight and clip key moments during calls
- Sync to HubSpot, Salesforce, Notion
- Supports 25+ languages
