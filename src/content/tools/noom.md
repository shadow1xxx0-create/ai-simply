Noom takes a psychology-first approach to weight loss, using AI to personalize behavior change programs. Rather than just tracking calories, Noom teaches you the psychology behind eating habits. AI coaches adapt your daily lessons, food logging goals, and check-ins based on your progress and psychology profile.

## Key Features

- Personalized psychology-based weight loss program
- AI coach that adapts to your progress
- Food logging with a traffic light color system
- Daily educational lessons on eating psychology
- Human coaching access
- Progress tracking and milestone celebrations

## Pricing

- **Monthly ($70/month)**: Full program access
- **Annual plans**: Discounted long-term pricing
- **Free trial**: 2-week trial available

## Best For

People who have tried traditional diets without success and want to address the psychological and behavioral root causes of their eating habits with AI-personalized support.
