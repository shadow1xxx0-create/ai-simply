Khanmigo is Khan Academy's AI-powered tutoring system, built with safety for students in mind. Rather than just giving answers, it guides students through problems with Socratic questioning — helping them understand concepts rather than just getting the right answer. Teachers get AI lesson planning, rubric creation, and progress tracking tools.

## Key Features

- Socratic tutoring method (guides, doesn't just answer)
- Covers all Khan Academy subjects (math, science, SAT prep)
- Debate partner and essay writing coach
- Teacher tools for lesson plans and rubrics
- Safe, age-appropriate responses
- Parent dashboard for progress monitoring

## Pricing

- **Free for students and teachers** (US, limited availability)
- **Individual ($9/month)**: Full access for personal use

## Best For

K-12 students who need personalized help with homework and test prep, and teachers who want AI assistance for lesson planning and differentiated instruction.
