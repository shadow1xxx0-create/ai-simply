SeaArt is a free AI art generation platform powered by Stable Diffusion with a large library of community models and LoRAs. It specializes in anime, fantasy, and character art — making it popular among digital artists and game designers. The platform hosts a large community where users share models, prompts, and artwork.

## Key Features

- Stable Diffusion-based generation with custom models
- Anime, fantasy, and realistic art styles
- Large library of community LoRAs and checkpoints
- Inpainting and outpainting tools
- Batch generation for multiple variations
- Community model sharing and discovery

## Pricing

- **Free**: 150 credits/day (~15 images)
- **Standard ($9.9/month)**: 3,000 credits/month
- **Pro ($19.9/month)**: 8,000 credits/month

## Best For

Anime and fantasy artists, game designers, and Stable Diffusion enthusiasts who want access to a large library of community models and free daily generations.
