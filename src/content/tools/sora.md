Sora is OpenAI's video generation model that produces high-quality, cinematic video clips from text prompts. Available to ChatGPT Plus and Pro subscribers, Sora can generate videos up to 20 seconds long with realistic physics, complex camera movements, and consistent characters across scenes. Also supports image-to-video and video extension.

## Key Features

- Text-to-video up to 20 seconds
- Image to video animation
- Video extension (make existing videos longer)
- Storyboard mode for multi-scene control
- Remix — transform existing videos
- Blend mode for combining video concepts

## Pricing

- **ChatGPT Plus ($20/month)**: 50 priority/month, 480p, 5 sec
- **ChatGPT Pro ($200/month)**: Unlimited priority, 1080p, 20 sec

## Best For

Content creators, marketers, and filmmakers who want to generate high-quality video clips quickly for social media, advertising, and creative projects.
