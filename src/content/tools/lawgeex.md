LawGeex uses AI to review contracts automatically against your company's predefined legal standards and playbook. It identifies risky clauses, flags deviations, and generates redlines in minutes — work that would take a lawyer hours. Used by Fortune 500 legal teams to review NDAs, vendor agreements, and procurement contracts at scale.

## Key Features

- Automated contract review against custom playbook
- AI clause identification and risk flagging
- Automatic redlining and suggested edits
- Works with Word, PDF, and DocuSign documents
- Integrates with CLM systems (Salesforce, Ironclad)
- Audit trail and approval workflows
