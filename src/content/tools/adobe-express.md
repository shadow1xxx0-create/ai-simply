Adobe Express (formerly Adobe Spark) is a simplified design tool that makes professional graphic design accessible to everyone. Powered by Adobe's AI, it can generate text effects, remove backgrounds, resize for any platform, and create videos — all through a drag-and-drop interface with thousands of professional templates.

## Key Features

- 35,000+ templates for every platform and format
- AI text effects and generative fill
- One-click background removal
- Brand kit for consistent visual identity
- Video creation with transitions and music
- Resize content for any platform instantly

## Pricing

- **Free**: Core features, Adobe fonts, templates
- **Premium ($9.99/month)**: All premium templates, AI features, fonts
- **Included with Creative Cloud**: Full access

## Best For

Small business owners, social media managers, students, and anyone who needs professional-looking designs quickly without advanced design skills.
