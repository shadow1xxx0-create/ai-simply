Albert is an autonomous AI marketing platform that manages and optimizes paid media campaigns across Google, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, and Bing — without constant human intervention. It analyzes performance data in real time, shifts budget to winning audiences and creatives, and runs thousands of micro-tests simultaneously to maximize ROI.

## Key Features

- Autonomous campaign management across channels
- Real-time budget optimization and reallocation
- Audience discovery and micro-segmentation
- Creative performance testing at scale
- Cross-channel attribution and reporting
- Integrates with existing ad accounts

## Pricing

- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing based on ad spend (contact sales)

## Best For

Mid-market and enterprise marketing teams managing significant paid media budgets who want AI to autonomously optimize campaigns across channels and free up strategists for higher-level work.
