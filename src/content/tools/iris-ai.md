Iris.ai is an AI research workspace designed for deep scientific work. Upload hundreds of papers and Iris extracts key findings, synthesizes across documents, and answers complex research questions with evidence. Used by R&D teams and enterprises to accelerate technology scouting, competitive intelligence, and literature reviews.

## Key Features

- Bulk paper upload and processing
- AI synthesis across multiple documents
- Evidence extraction with source tracking
- Technology scouting workflows
- Research question answering with citations
- Team workspace for collaborative research

## Pricing

- **Free**: 10 papers/month, basic features
- **Researcher ($29/month)**: 100 papers, full AI features
- **Enterprise**: Unlimited, API, custom deployment

## Best For

R&D teams, technology scouts, and enterprise researchers who need to synthesize insights across hundreds of scientific papers quickly — beyond what any individual could read manually.
