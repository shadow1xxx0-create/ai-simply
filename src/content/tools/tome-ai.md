Tome generates complete, professional presentations from a single prompt. Describe what you need — a pitch deck, a product overview, a sales proposal — and it produces a full narrative presentation with slides, images, and copy. The interactive tiles let you embed live data from Figma, Twitter, and video tools directly into slides.

## Key Features

- AI generates full presentations from a prompt in seconds
- Smart layouts that adapt to your content automatically
- Live tile embeds (Figma, Loom, Twitter, maps, code)
- AI image generation for visuals inside slides
- Shareable link — no download needed for presenting
- AI outline and rewrite tools for refining drafts

## Pricing

- **Free**: 500 AI credits, unlimited pages
- **Pro ($16/month)**: Unlimited AI, custom fonts, analytics
- **Enterprise**: Custom branding, SSO, admin controls

## Best For

Founders doing pitch decks, sales teams building proposals, and anyone who needs a polished presentation fast without fighting with slide design.
