Intercom is a leading customer communication platform that has gone all-in on AI. Fin, their AI agent, is trained on your help center content and can resolve over 50% of customer queries without human involvement. The platform also includes shared inbox, live chat, product tours, and customer data management.

## Key Features

- Fin AI agent — autonomous support resolution
- Shared inbox for human support escalation
- Live chat, email, SMS, and WhatsApp
- Help center with AI-powered article suggestions
- Customer data and CRM
- Proactive messaging and product tours

## Pricing

- **Essential ($39/month)**: 1 seat, Fin AI ($0.99/resolution)
- **Advanced ($99/month)**: 1 seat, advanced automation
- **Expert ($139/month)**: Workload management, reports

## Best For

SaaS companies, e-commerce brands, and growing startups that want to scale customer support without proportionally scaling headcount.
