Connected Papers builds a visual graph of academic papers related to any research topic. Enter a paper you know, and it generates a network of connected work based on citation relationships — making it easy to discover prior research, identify key papers, and build comprehensive literature reviews without manually chasing references.

## Key Features

- Visual graph of related academic papers
- Citation and reference relationship mapping
- Prior works and derivative works views
- Paper summaries and abstracts inline
- Export to reference managers
- No academic login required to explore

## Pricing

- **Free**: 5 graphs/month
- **Academic ($3/month)**: Unlimited graphs, faster generation
- **Pro ($6/month)**: Priority processing, full history

## Best For

Researchers, PhD students, and academics who need to quickly map a field, discover related work, and build literature reviews without spending hours chasing individual citations.
