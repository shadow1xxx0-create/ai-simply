LegalZoom is the most widely used online legal service, making basic legal tasks accessible without expensive attorneys. Their AI helps you form LLCs, draft contracts, create wills, register trademarks, and handle other routine legal documents. Attorney review is available as an add-on for more complex situations.

## Key Features

- LLC and corporation formation (all 50 states)
- Business contracts and legal agreements
- Wills, trusts, and estate planning
- Trademark registration
- Attorney access on-demand
- Registered agent services

## Pricing

- **Business Formation (from $0 + state fees)**: LLC/Corp filing
- **Legal Advantage ($9.99/month)**: Unlimited document updates, attorney Q&A
- **Attorney consultations**: Pay-per-session

## Best For

Small business owners, entrepreneurs, freelancers, and individuals who need basic legal documents and business formation without the cost of a traditional law firm.
