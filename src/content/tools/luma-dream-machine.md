Luma Dream Machine (Ray2) generates smooth, high-quality video clips known for their realistic motion and physics. Unlike some competitors, it handles complex scenes with multiple objects well and generates notably fluid movement. Fast generation (under 2 minutes) and clean, simple interface make it accessible to beginners and professionals.

## Key Features

- Text to video with realistic physics
- Image to video animation
- Keyframe control for precise scene direction
- Camera control (orbit, push, pull, etc.)
- Loop generation for seamless videos
- API for developers

## Pricing

- **Free**: 30 generations/month (720p, watermarked)
- **Standard ($29.99/month)**: 120 generations, 1080p
- **Pro ($99.99/month)**: 400 generations, priority
- **Premier ($499.99/month)**: 2,000 generations

## Best For

Content creators, advertisers, and filmmakers who need fast, high-quality video generation with realistic movement — especially for product visualizations and social media content.
