Yummly uses AI to learn your food preferences, dietary restrictions, and cooking habits — then surfaces personalized recipe recommendations from 2 million+ recipes. The meal planner generates weekly menus and auto-creates shopping lists. Yummly Pro offers step-by-step guided cooking with video instructions.

## Key Features

- AI-personalized recipe recommendations
- Dietary filter (vegan, keto, gluten-free, allergies)
- Weekly meal planner with auto shopping lists
- Guided cooking mode with step-by-step video
- Smart grocery list synced to meal plan
- 2 million+ recipes from top food sites

## Pricing

- **Free**: Recipe discovery, meal planning basics
- **Yummly Pro ($4.99/month)**: Guided cooking, ad-free

## Best For

Home cooks who want personalized recipe recommendations, people with specific dietary requirements, and meal preppers who want a streamlined planning and shopping experience.
