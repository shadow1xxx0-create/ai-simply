Hypotenuse AI is built for teams that need high-volume content — primarily e-commerce businesses writing product descriptions and content marketing teams producing blog posts at scale. Its bulk generation tools can produce hundreds of unique, SEO-optimized descriptions from a spreadsheet.

## Key Features

- Bulk product description generator via CSV import
- Blog Wizard — research, outline, draft, and optimize in one flow
- Brand voice training from existing content
- HypoDoc — chat with your documents and knowledge base
- Shopify and WooCommerce integrations
- Plagiarism checker built in

## Pricing

- **Free trial**: 7 days, limited credits
- **Individual ($29/month)**: 100K words, 1 user
- **Teams ($59/month)**: 400K words, brand voices, team access
- **Enterprise**: Custom volume and API access

## Best For

E-commerce brands with large product catalogs, content marketing teams that need high-volume blog and SEO content, and agencies managing multiple client accounts.
