PicPurify is an AI-powered image moderation API that detects inappropriate content — including nudity, violence, hate symbols, and drug-related imagery — in real time. It's designed for platforms with user-generated content who need automated moderation without building their own vision models.

## Key Features

- NSFW and explicit content detection
- Violence and gore classification
- Hate symbol and extremist content detection
- Drug and weapon identification
- Real-time API with fast response
- Customizable sensitivity thresholds

## Pricing

- **Free**: 500 images/month
- **Starter ($29/month)**: 10,000 images/month
- **Growth ($99/month)**: 50,000 images/month
- **Enterprise**: Custom volume

## Best For

App developers and platform operators who need reliable automated image moderation to keep user-generated content safe — without building and maintaining their own vision models.
