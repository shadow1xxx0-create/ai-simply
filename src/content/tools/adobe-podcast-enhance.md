Podcastle is an all-in-one AI podcast creation platform that lets anyone record, edit, and publish professional-quality audio without a studio. Its AI Magic Dust feature enhances audio quality in one click — removing background noise, leveling volume, and making cheap microphones sound professional. It also transcribes episodes automatically for show notes and captions.

## Key Features

- One-click AI audio enhancement (Magic Dust)
- Browser-based recording — no software install
- Automatic transcription and show notes
- Multi-track remote recording with guests
- Text-based audio editing
- Revoice — clone your voice for corrections

## Pricing

- **Free**: 3 hours of recording/month, basic features
- **Storyteller ($11.99/month)**: 10 hours, full AI features
- **Pro ($23.99/month)**: Unlimited recording, team features

## Best For

Podcasters, content creators, and businesses who want to produce professional audio without expensive equipment or editing skills — using AI to handle the technical work automatically.
