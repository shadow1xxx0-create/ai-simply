Ada is a medical AI app that guides you through a detailed symptom assessment and provides an evidence-based health report — with possible causes ranked by likelihood. Built with medical doctors and used by 13 million people worldwide, Ada helps users understand their symptoms and decide whether to seek care.

## Key Features

- Guided symptom questionnaire
- Evidence-based condition assessment
- Ranked list of possible causes
- Advice on urgency of care
- Health history tracking
- Available in 10 languages

## Pricing

- **Free**: Personal symptom checker
- **Enterprise**: API and B2B health system integration

## Best For

Anyone experiencing symptoms and wanting a reliable first assessment before (or instead of) searching online — especially for non-emergency health questions.
