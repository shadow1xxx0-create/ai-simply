QuillBot is the most popular AI paraphrasing tool, used by over 35 million people. Paste any text and it rewrites it — changing vocabulary and structure while keeping the original meaning. Multiple modes (Standard, Fluency, Formal, Creative, Shorten) let you control the output style. Also includes a summarizer, grammar checker, and citation generator.

## Key Features

- 8 paraphrasing modes (Standard, Formal, Creative, Shorten, etc.)
- Word flipper for synonym suggestions
- AI Summarizer for long documents
- Grammar checker with suggestions
- Citation generator (APA, MLA, Chicago)
- Chrome extension for browser-wide use

## Pricing

- **Free**: 125 words at a time, 3 paraphrase modes
- **Premium ($9.95/month)**: Unlimited words, all modes, freeze words

## Best For

Students, ESL writers, content creators, and academics who need to paraphrase text, avoid plagiarism, improve clarity, or adapt writing to different styles and tones.
