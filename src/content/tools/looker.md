Looker is Google Cloud's enterprise business intelligence platform that lets teams explore, analyze, and visualize data without writing SQL. With AI-powered natural language queries, you can ask questions in plain English and get instant charts and insights. Used by data teams at thousands of companies to build self-serve analytics.

## Key Features

- Natural language querying — ask questions in plain English
- Drag-and-drop dashboard builder
- LookML modeling layer for governed metrics
- Embedded analytics for customer-facing products
- Integration with 50+ data sources
- Scheduled reports and alerts

## Pricing

- **Standard**: From $5,000/month (contact sales)
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing
- **Embed**: Custom pricing for embedded analytics

## Best For

Data teams and enterprises who need governed, self-serve business intelligence with AI-assisted querying built on top of their existing data warehouse.
