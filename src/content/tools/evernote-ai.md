Evernote has been capturing ideas, web clips, and documents for over 15 years. Recent AI updates add natural language search, automatic summarization, and writing assistance — bringing the granddaddy of note apps into the AI era.

## Key Features

- AI Search — ask questions across your entire note library
- AI Summarize — condense long notes and documents instantly
- Web Clipper browser extension saves articles, recipes, and PDFs
- Document scanning from mobile camera
- Tasks and reminders integrated with notes
- Offline access on all plans

## Pricing

- **Free**: 1 notebook, 50 notes, 60MB uploads/month
- **Personal ($14.99/month)**: Unlimited notebooks, 10GB uploads, AI features
- **Professional ($17.99/month)**: Unlimited AI, advanced search, tasks

## Best For

People with years of accumulated notes who want AI to make their existing library searchable, or users who need reliable cross-device sync and web clipping.
