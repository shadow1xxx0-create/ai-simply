Duolingo uses AI to personalize language learning across 40+ languages. With Duolingo Max, learners get Roleplay — AI conversations with characters in real-life scenarios — and Explain My Answer, which gives in-depth grammar explanations. The gamified format and daily streaks have made it the world's most downloaded education app.

## Key Features

- 40+ languages including Japanese, Mandarin, Arabic
- AI Roleplay for real conversation practice (Max)
- Personalized lesson pacing based on performance
- Duolingo for Schools for classrooms
- Listening and speaking exercises
- Daily streak tracking and gamification

## Pricing

- **Free**: All lessons with ads
- **Super Duolingo ($7/month)**: No ads, unlimited hearts
- **Duolingo Max ($14/month)**: AI Roleplay and Explain My Answer

## Best For

Anyone learning a new language, from complete beginners to intermediate learners who want daily practice in a fun, low-commitment format.
