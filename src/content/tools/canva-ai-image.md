Canva's AI Image Generator lets you create original images from text prompts directly inside the Canva design editor. Generate a visual, then immediately drop it into a social post, presentation, or flyer — no switching between apps. It's the easiest way to get AI-generated images into finished designs for non-designers.

## Key Features

- Text-to-image generation inside Canva editor
- Multiple style options (photo, illustration, watercolor, etc.)
- Instantly usable in any Canva template or design
- Magic Edit to modify existing images with AI
- Background remover and image enhancer
- Included in Canva Pro subscription

## Pricing

- **Free**: Limited AI image generations per month
- **Pro ($15/month)**: 500 AI image generations/month
- **Teams ($10/user/month)**: Shared brand assets and generations

## Best For

Non-designers who want AI-generated images they can immediately use in social media posts, presentations, and marketing materials — all without leaving Canva.
