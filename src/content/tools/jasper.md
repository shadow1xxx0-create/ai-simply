Jasper is an enterprise-focused AI writing tool built for marketing teams. It learns your brand voice, integrates with your marketing stack, and helps teams create on-brand blog posts, ad copy, email campaigns, and social content at scale. Jasper is used by teams at IBM, Airbnb, and other major brands.

## Key Features

- Brand Voice — AI learns your tone and style
- 50+ marketing templates (ads, emails, blogs)
- SEO mode with SurferSEO integration
- Team collaboration and workflows
- Chrome extension for writing anywhere
- Jasper Art for image generation

## Pricing

- **Creator ($39/month)**: Single user, 1 brand voice
- **Teams ($99/month)**: 3 users, 3 brand voices
- **Business**: Custom pricing, advanced features

## Best For

Marketing teams, content agencies, and enterprise brands who need to produce consistent, on-brand content at volume.
