Planner 5D is an intuitive home design tool that lets you create detailed 2D floor plans and instantly view them in 3D. The AI Design Generator can create room layouts from a text description. With 5,000+ furniture and decor items, it's used by homeowners, interior designers, and architects for space planning.

## Key Features

- AI room generator from text descriptions
- 2D and 3D floor plan creation
- 5,000+ furniture and decor catalog
- Photorealistic 4K renders
- Smart resize for furniture fitting
- Virtual reality view mode

## Pricing

- **Free**: Basic features, limited items
- **Basic ($7.99/month)**: Full catalog, HD renders
- **Pro ($12.99/month)**: 4K renders, unlimited projects

## Best For

Homeowners planning renovations, interior designers creating client presentations, and anyone who wants to plan a room layout and visualize furniture placement in 3D before buying.
