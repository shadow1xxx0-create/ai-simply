Stable Diffusion is the most popular open-source image generation model, developed by Stability AI. It can be run entirely locally on your own GPU, giving you complete privacy, no usage limits, and full creative control. The community has built thousands of fine-tuned models, LoRAs, and extensions through platforms like CivitAI and Automatic1111.

## Key Features

- Completely free and open-source
- Run locally — no API costs or limits
- Thousands of community fine-tuned models
- ControlNet for precise composition control
- Inpainting, outpainting, and img2img
- SDXL and SD3 for higher quality outputs

## Pricing

- **Free**: Download and run locally
- **Stability AI API**: Pay-per-image for cloud inference
- **DreamStudio**: Credits-based web interface

## Best For

Developers, power users, and creators who want unlimited generations, full control, and privacy — and have a capable GPU.
