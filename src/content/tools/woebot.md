Woebot is an AI mental health app developed at Stanford that delivers Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) techniques through conversation. It helps users identify negative thought patterns, manage stress and anxiety, and build emotional resilience. Woebot is not a replacement for professional therapy but is evidence-based and clinically validated.

## Key Features

- CBT and DBT-based conversational exercises
- Mood tracking and pattern identification
- Guided breathing and relaxation exercises
- Personalized check-ins
- Available 24/7, no appointment needed
- Backed by peer-reviewed clinical research

## Pricing

- **Free**: Core app features
- **Enterprise/B2B**: Clinical partnerships

## Best For

People experiencing mild-to-moderate anxiety or stress who want evidence-based mental health support between therapy sessions or as a first step toward care.
