Elicit is an AI research tool purpose-built for academic and scientific research. It searches through millions of papers, extracts key data points, and helps you conduct literature reviews, synthesize findings, and identify research gaps. Built for researchers who need reliable, evidence-based information.

## Key Features

- Search 200M+ academic papers
- Extract data from papers automatically
- Lit review synthesis across multiple papers
- Find related work and citations
- Export to CSV for systematic reviews
- Ask questions about any paper
