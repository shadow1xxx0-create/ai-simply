Perplexity's Deep Research feature conducts autonomous, multi-step web research on complex topics. Give it a research question and it independently searches dozens of sources, analyzes results, and produces a comprehensive, fully-cited report. One of the most capable AI research tools available today.

## Key Features

- Autonomous multi-step research process
- Cites every claim with source links
- Produces structured research reports
- Works on complex, nuanced topics
- Export to PDF or share link
- Builds on conversational follow-ups
