Power BI is Microsoft's business intelligence platform, deeply integrated with the Microsoft 365 ecosystem. Copilot in Power BI lets you ask questions in natural language and get instant charts and reports. AI visuals include anomaly detection, decomposition trees, and smart narratives that automatically explain data trends.

## Key Features

- Copilot — generate reports from natural language
- Smart Narratives for automatic data storytelling
- Anomaly detection on time series data
- Q&A visual for conversational data exploration
- Decomposition tree for root cause analysis
- 200+ data connectors (Salesforce, Excel, SQL, etc.)

## Pricing

- **Free**: Power BI Desktop app, basic sharing
- **Power BI Pro ($10/user/month)**: Full sharing and collaboration
- **Premium ($20/user/month)**: AI features, Copilot, larger datasets
- **Premium Per Capacity**: Enterprise infrastructure

## Best For

Business analysts, data teams, and Microsoft 365 organizations who want to build interactive dashboards and use AI to surface insights from their business data.
