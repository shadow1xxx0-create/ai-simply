HubSpot has embedded AI throughout its entire platform — from AI content writer and social media caption generator to predictive lead scoring and AI email personalization. With Breeze AI, HubSpot can research prospects, enrich contact data, and automate entire marketing and sales workflows end-to-end.

## Key Features

- Breeze AI for content, prospecting, and data enrichment
- AI blog post and landing page writer
- Predictive lead scoring with machine learning
- AI email personalization at scale
- Social media caption and ad copy generator
- AI chatbot builder for websites

## Pricing

- **Free**: AI writing tools, CRM, basic features
- **Starter ($20/month)**: Remove HubSpot branding, more AI credits
- **Professional ($800/month)**: Full marketing automation
- **Enterprise ($3,600/month)**: Advanced AI, custom objects

## Best For

Marketing and sales teams at growing businesses who want a unified AI-powered platform for inbound marketing, CRM, and customer communication.
