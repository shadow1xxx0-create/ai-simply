CivitAI is the GitHub of Stable Diffusion — the central repository where the community shares fine-tuned models, LoRAs, embeddings, and VAEs. With 100,000+ models and 3 billion+ image generations monthly, it's where you find specialized models for specific art styles, characters, and subjects. You can also run models directly on the platform.

## Key Features

- 100,000+ community-created models
- LoRA, checkpoint, and embedding downloads
- Run models directly on CivitAI (no setup)
- Community image gallery with prompts visible
- Model reviews and ratings
- Free monthly credits for on-site generation

## Pricing

- **Free**: Browse, download, and use models; limited on-site generation
- **Buzz Credits**: Buy for more on-site generations

## Best For

Stable Diffusion users looking for specialized fine-tuned models, anime artists, character designers, and anyone who wants to explore the creative output of the AI art community.
