Playground AI offers a powerful AI image creation and editing environment with a canvas-based interface similar to Canva or Photoshop — but powered by AI. The generous free tier (500 images/day on free filters) makes it one of the most accessible AI image tools available. Features include inpainting, outpainting, and an AI photo editor.

## Key Features

- 500 free images per day on free models
- Canvas editor for compositing and editing
- Inpainting and outpainting tools
- Stable Diffusion and custom model support
- Image filters and style presets
- Real-time collaborative canvas

## Pricing

- **Free**: 500 images/day (free models), 50 images/day (premium)
- **Pro ($15/month)**: Unlimited free model images, 2,000 premium/day

## Best For

Designers, creators, and experimenters who want maximum flexibility and volume for AI image creation — especially those on a budget who want premium features for free.
