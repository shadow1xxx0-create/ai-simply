Udio is Suno's main competitor, known for producing especially rich, professional-sounding music. It offers fine-grained style tags, the ability to edit and extend tracks, and a strong community of shared creations. The audio quality on Udio's outputs is widely considered among the best in AI music generation.

## Key Features

- High-quality audio output
- Fine-grained genre and instrument tags
- Inpainting — edit specific sections of a track
- Extend songs in either direction
- Remix and reinterpret tracks
- Large community discovery feed

## Pricing

- **Free**: 100 credits/month (~10 songs)
- **Standard ($10/month)**: 1,200 credits
- **Pro ($30/month)**: 4,800 credits, commercial use

## Best For

Musicians and producers who want more control over their AI-generated music and the highest possible audio quality.
