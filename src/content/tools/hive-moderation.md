Hive Moderation provides AI-powered content moderation APIs for detecting AI-generated images, deepfakes, NSFW content, hate speech, and spam at scale. Used by major platforms and apps, it processes billions of content items monthly — giving trust and safety teams the AI tools to moderate user-generated content without building models in-house.

## Key Features

- AI-generated image detection (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion)
- Deepfake detection for photos and video
- NSFW and explicit content classification
- Hate speech and toxicity detection
- Spam and fraud identification
- Real-time API with sub-100ms response
