Mem is an AI-first notes app that organizes itself — you just write and the AI finds connections, suggests related notes, and surfaces relevant information when you need it. Chat with your notes (similar to ChatGPT on your personal knowledge base) to quickly extract insights and information from everything you've written.

## Key Features

- AI-powered automatic organization (no folders needed)
- Chat with your notes for instant retrieval
- Smart suggestions for related content
- Full-text search across all notes
- Capture from email, Slack, and web
- Collaborative shared spaces

## Pricing

- **Free**: Core note-taking, limited AI
- **Pro ($14.99/month)**: Full AI features, unlimited notes

## Best For

Knowledge workers, researchers, and consultants who accumulate large amounts of notes and information and want AI to surface relevant connections rather than manually organizing everything.
