Grok is built by Elon Musk's xAI and is integrated with X (formerly Twitter). It has real-time access to what's trending on X, making it uniquely good at current events and social media context. Grok 3 is one of the most capable models available and is now accessible on the free tier.

## Key Features

- Real-time X (Twitter) data and trending topics
- Grok 3 model — competitive with GPT-4o and Claude
- DeepSearch for thorough web research
- Image generation built in
- Less restricted responses than competitors
- Available in the X mobile app

## Pricing

- **Free**: Grok 3, limited daily usage
- **X Premium ($8/month)**: Increased limits
- **X Premium+ ($16/month)**: Full access

## Best For

X power users, news junkies, and anyone who wants AI with access to real-time social media conversations and a more direct communication style.
