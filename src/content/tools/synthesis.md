Synthesis started as an after-school program for children of SpaceX employees and expanded into an AI-powered learning platform. It uses adaptive games and simulations to teach math, critical thinking, and problem-solving — adjusting difficulty in real time to keep kids challenged without frustration. Now available to families worldwide.

## Key Features

- Adaptive AI adjusts to each child's level
- Math and problem-solving games and simulations
- Collaborative challenges with other students
- Weekly progress reports for parents
- Teaches strategic thinking, not just memorization
- Used by 50,000+ students globally

## Pricing

- **Individual ($35/month)**: One child, full access
- **Family**: Sibling discounts available

## Best For

Parents who want their kids to develop strong math and critical thinking skills through engaging, adaptive AI-powered games — not boring drills or passive video lessons.
