Eat This Much is an AI-powered meal planning tool that automatically generates personalized meal plans based on your calorie goals, dietary restrictions, and food preferences. Set your target macros and it builds a full week of meals — then generates a grocery list with exact quantities. It removes the daily "what should I eat?" decision completely.

## Key Features

- Automatic weekly meal plan generation
- Calorie and macro goal customization
- Dietary filters (vegan, keto, paleo, gluten-free, etc.)
- Auto-generated grocery lists with quantities
- Thousands of recipes with nutrition info
- Schedule meals around your routine

## Pricing

- **Free**: Daily meal planning, limited customization
- **Premium ($9/month)**: Weekly plans, full customization, grocery lists

## Best For

Anyone who wants to eat healthier without spending time deciding what to cook — the AI handles all meal planning and grocery shopping based on your nutrition goals.
