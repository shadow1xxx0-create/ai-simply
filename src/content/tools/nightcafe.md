NightCafe is one of the oldest and most community-driven AI art platforms. It supports multiple AI models (Stable Diffusion, DALL-E 3, and others) and has a thriving social community where users share and vote on creations. The credit system is generous — you earn free credits daily and through social interaction.

## Key Features

- Multiple AI models (SD, DALL-E 3, SDXL)
- Large social community for sharing and discovery
- Daily free credits (no card required)
- Bulk image generation
- Print and merchandise ordering
- 30+ unique art styles

## Pricing

- **Free**: 5 credits/day (enough for 1-5 images)
- **AI Hobbyist ($6/month)**: 100 credits/month
- **AI Enthusiast ($10/month)**: 200 credits/month
- **AI Artist ($20/month)**: 500 credits/month

## Best For

Hobbyists, digital art enthusiasts, and anyone who wants to experiment with AI art and connect with a creative community without spending much.
