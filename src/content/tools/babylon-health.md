Babylon Health is a digital healthcare company that combines AI symptom checking with access to real doctors through video consultations. The AI triage system assesses symptoms, checks risk factors, and guides users to the appropriate level of care — from self-care advice to urgent referrals. Available in multiple countries as both consumer and enterprise health solutions.

## Key Features

- AI symptom checker with clinical-grade accuracy
- On-demand video GP consultations
- Prescription delivery to your pharmacy
- Health monitoring and chronic condition management
- Integration with NHS (UK) and insurance plans
- Personal health record

## Pricing

- **Free**: Symptom checking, health articles
- **Consultation fees**: Variable by region and provider
- **Enterprise**: Employer and insurance partnerships

## Best For

Patients who want quick access to medical advice, people in areas with limited GP availability, and employers providing digital health benefits to employees.
