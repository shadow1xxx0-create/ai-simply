Photomath lets you scan any math problem with your camera and instantly shows the step-by-step solution with explanations. It works for everything from basic arithmetic to calculus. Unlike calculators that just give answers, Photomath explains each step — making it a learning tool, not just a homework shortcut.

## Key Features

- Camera scan for handwritten and printed problems
- Step-by-step solutions with explanations
- Multiple solving methods shown
- Covers arithmetic, algebra, geometry, calculus
- Animated tutorials for each solution step
- Offline functionality

## Pricing

- **Free**: Scanning and basic solutions
- **Photomath Plus ($9.99/month)**: Detailed explanations, animated tutorials

## Best For

Students from middle school through university who struggle with math and want to understand how problems are solved, not just get the answers.
