Wolfram Alpha is a computational knowledge engine that can answer factual questions, solve complex equations, generate data visualizations, and provide step-by-step solutions across math, science, engineering, and data analysis. It's the backbone of many apps (including Siri for math) and remains the gold standard for computational problem-solving.

## Key Features

- Step-by-step math solutions (calculus, linear algebra)
- Chemistry, physics, and engineering calculations
- Data analysis and visualization
- Natural language queries
- Wolfram Language integration
- ChatGPT plugin available

## Pricing

- **Free**: Standard queries with basic results
- **Pro ($7.25/month)**: Step-by-step solutions, extended computation
- **Pro Premium ($12/month)**: Full power, priority computation

## Best For

STEM students, engineers, scientists, and data professionals who need reliable, precise computational answers to complex mathematical and scientific questions.
