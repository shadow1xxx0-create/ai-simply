Amplitude is an enterprise-grade digital analytics platform focused on product-led growth. Its AI features include Amplitude AI (natural language queries), predictive cohorts (identify users likely to convert or churn), and automated insight recommendations. Trusted by 3,000+ companies including Dropbox, Walmart, and PayPal.

## Key Features

- Amplitude AI for natural language data exploration
- Predictive cohorts for conversion and churn
- Experiment (A/B testing platform)
- Session Replay for qualitative insights
- Real-time streaming analytics
- Customer Data Platform (CDP)

## Pricing

- **Free**: 50K monthly tracked users, core analytics
- **Plus ($61/month)**: 100K users, advanced charts
- **Growth**: Custom pricing, enterprise features
- **Enterprise**: Unlimited, dedicated support

## Best For

Enterprise product teams, growth marketers, and data analysts who need sophisticated behavioral analytics, experimentation, and AI-powered predictions for digital products.
