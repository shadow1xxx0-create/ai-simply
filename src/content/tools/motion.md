Motion uses AI to automatically plan your day. It takes all your tasks with deadlines, your meetings from Google/Outlook calendar, and your working hours — then builds the optimal schedule for you. When something changes (a meeting gets added, a task is delayed), it replans your entire day automatically.

## Key Features

- AI auto-scheduling of tasks around meetings
- Automatic replanning when priorities change
- Project and deadline management
- Google Calendar and Outlook sync
- Meeting scheduling links
- Team calendar coordination

## Pricing

- **Individual ($34/month)**: Full AI scheduling
- **Team ($20/user/month)**: Shared projects, team planning

## Best For

Busy professionals, managers, and anyone who struggles to find time for deep work among constant meetings and competing priorities.
