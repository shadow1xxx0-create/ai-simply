Hemingway Editor is a writing tool focused on clarity and readability. It highlights hard-to-read sentences, passive voice, adverb overuse, and complex word choices — then suggests simpler alternatives. The readability score shows the grade level required to understand your writing. Simple, focused, and effective.

## Key Features

- Color-coded sentence complexity highlighting
- Passive voice detection
- Adverb and complex word flagging
- Flesch-Kincaid readability grade level
- Word count and estimated reading time
- AI-powered rewrite suggestions (AI mode)

## Pricing

- **Free**: Web app, unlimited use
- **Desktop App ($19.99 one-time)**: Offline use, distraction-free mode
- **AI Mode ($10/month)**: AI-powered rewrites

## Best For

Content writers, bloggers, journalists, and business writers who want their writing to be clear, direct, and accessible — especially useful for simplifying technical or academic writing.
