Quizlet is the world's most popular study tool, used by 300 million students and teachers. The AI (Q-Chat) generates flashcards, practice tests, and study guides from any material you upload. Magic Notes turns lecture notes and textbooks into complete study sets automatically — saving hours of manual flashcard creation.

## Key Features

- Magic Notes — AI generates flashcards from your notes
- Q-Chat AI tutor for personalized study sessions
- Spaced repetition with adaptive learning
- Practice tests, learn mode, and match games
- Class sharing and teacher-created sets
- 700 million+ community study sets

## Pricing

- **Free**: 20 AI flashcard sets/month, core study modes
- **Quizlet Plus ($35.99/year)**: Unlimited AI, offline access
- **Teacher ($35.99/year)**: Class management tools

## Best For

Students at all levels who want AI-assisted flashcard creation, personalized study sessions, and adaptive practice to prepare for exams efficiently.
