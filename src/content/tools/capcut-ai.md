CapCut is the world's most downloaded free video editor, used by 200 million creators monthly. Its AI features include auto-captions (auto-generated subtitles), AI background removal, AI noise cancellation, smart cutouts, and hundreds of trend-driven templates. The desktop, web, and mobile apps are all free.

## Key Features

- Auto-captions with 99% accuracy in 25+ languages
- AI background removal (no green screen)
- AI noise cancellation for audio
- One-click viral templates (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
- Text-to-video feature
- AI voice changer and vocal enhancer

## Pricing

- **Free**: Almost all features, minor export limits
- **Pro ($7.99/month)**: 4K export, cloud storage, premium assets

## Best For

Content creators, TikTokers, Reels producers, and anyone who wants a powerful, free video editing tool with AI features for creating social media content quickly.
