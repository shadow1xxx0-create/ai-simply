K Health uses AI trained on millions of real patient cases to help you understand your symptoms and connect with licensed doctors. The AI provides an initial assessment, then a real physician reviews and can prescribe medication if needed — all through the app. It covers primary care, mental health, and chronic condition management.

## Key Features

- AI symptom checker trained on real medical data
- Licensed physician review and consultation
- Prescription delivery to your pharmacy
- Unlimited primary care visits on subscription
- Mental health support and medication management
- Chronic condition monitoring (hypertension, diabetes)

## Pricing

- **Free**: Symptom checker, one free visit
- **K Health Unlimited ($49/month)**: Unlimited primary care visits
- **Mental Health ($69/month)**: Psychiatry and therapy access

## Best For

People who want fast, affordable access to primary care and prescriptions through AI-assisted consultations — without long wait times or expensive in-person visits.
