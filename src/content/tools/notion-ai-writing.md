Anyword is an AI copywriting tool that doesn't just generate text — it predicts how well it will perform. Every piece of copy gets a predictive performance score based on billions of data points, so you can pick the version most likely to convert. Used by marketers to write ads, emails, landing pages, and social posts that actually drive results.

## Key Features

- Predictive performance scoring on all copy
- Ad copy for Google, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn
- Email subject lines with open rate predictions
- Landing page and website copy generation
- Brand voice training and enforcement
- A/B testing copy variations

## Pricing

- **Free**: 2,500 words/month, performance scores
- **Starter ($39/month)**: 30,000 words, all channels
- **Data-Driven ($79/month)**: Unlimited words, custom scoring

## Best For

Performance marketers and growth teams who want AI copy that's optimized for conversion — not just fluent, but predicted to actually work based on real performance data.
