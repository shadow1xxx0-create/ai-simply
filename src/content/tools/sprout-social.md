Sprout Social is a professional social media management platform with AI features woven throughout. Its AI assists with optimal send times, content suggestions, automated responses, and social listening. Used by marketing teams at thousands of companies, Sprout combines publishing, engagement, and analytics in one platform.

## Key Features

- AI-powered optimal send time recommendations
- Unified inbox for all social messages
- Social listening and brand monitoring
- Detailed analytics and performance reports
- Content calendar and approval workflows
- Competitor analysis and benchmarking

## Pricing

- **Standard ($249/month)**: 5 profiles, core features
- **Professional ($399/month)**: Unlimited profiles, AI features
- **Advanced ($499/month)**: Listening, automations
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing

## Best For

Marketing teams and agencies managing multiple brands or clients who need a professional-grade social media platform with deep analytics and team collaboration.
