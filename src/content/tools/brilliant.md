Brilliant teaches math, science, and computer science through interactive problem-solving rather than passive video watching. Its AI adapts to your knowledge level, identifies gaps, and serves personalized daily challenges. Topics range from algebra and calculus to neural networks and quantum computing — all explained through hands-on problems.

## Key Features

- Interactive lessons across 60+ courses
- AI-adaptive difficulty and personalized paths
- Daily challenges to build consistent habits
- Topics from arithmetic to advanced CS and AI
- Visual, hands-on problem-solving approach
- Mobile app for learning on the go

## Pricing

- **Free**: Limited daily problems and course previews
- **Premium ($24.99/month)**: Full access to all courses and features
- **Annual ($149.99/year)**: Best value for long-term learners

## Best For

Students, professionals, and curious adults who want to build real understanding of math, science, and CS concepts through interactive problem-solving — not passive video lectures.
