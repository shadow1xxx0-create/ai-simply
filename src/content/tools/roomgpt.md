RoomGPT lets you upload a photo of any room and see it redesigned in dozens of interior design styles instantly — from minimalist and Scandinavian to maximalist and bohemian. Popular with homeowners planning renovations, real estate agents staging properties, and interior designers showing clients options.

## Key Features

- Upload any room photo for AI redesign
- 30+ design styles (modern, rustic, industrial, etc.)
- Multiple room types (bedroom, kitchen, living room)
- Before/after comparison view
- Download high-resolution results
- No sign-up required for basic use

## Pricing

- **Free**: 3 room renders (with watermark)
- **Basic ($9.99/month)**: 100 renders/month
- **Pro ($29.99/month)**: Unlimited renders, no watermark

## Best For

Homeowners planning renovations, real estate agents, interior designers, and anyone who wants to visualize design changes before committing to them.
