Mistral AI is a French company building some of the most efficient open-source AI models available. Their models (Mistral Large, Mistral Small, Mixtral) are known for speed, strong coding ability, and European data privacy compliance. Le Chat is their consumer interface, and all models are available via API.

## Key Features

- Open-source models (Apache 2.0 license)
- GDPR-compliant data handling
- Mistral Large — flagship model for complex tasks
- Mistral Small — fast and cost-efficient
- No data retention by default
- Competitive API pricing

## Pricing

- **Free**: Le Chat with generous usage
- **API**: Pay-per-token, very competitive rates
- **Enterprise**: On-premise deployment options

## Best For

European businesses with data compliance requirements, developers wanting open-source models, and teams needing fast, cost-effective AI inference.
