Sapling combines AI content detection with grammar and writing quality improvements. Its detector identifies text generated by GPT-4, Claude, and other AI models, while also offering real-time grammar correction and writing suggestions. Widely used by content teams and enterprises who need both detection and writing polish in one tool.

## Key Features

- AI-generated text detection with confidence scores
- Real-time grammar and style corrections
- Autocomplete and snippet suggestions
- API for integrating detection into your own tools
- Chrome extension for in-browser use
- Team reporting and usage analytics
