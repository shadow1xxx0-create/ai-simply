Uizard lets anyone design apps and websites without design skills. Upload a hand-drawn sketch and it converts it into a digital wireframe, or describe your app in plain text and Uizard generates the UI. Built for founders, product managers, and non-designers who need to visualize ideas fast.

## Key Features

- Text-to-UI generation from plain descriptions
- Sketch-to-wireframe conversion via photo upload
- Screenshot scanner to clone and edit existing UIs
- Drag-and-drop editor with pre-built components
- Auto-layout and responsive design
- Team collaboration and sharing

## Pricing

- **Free**: 2 projects, limited screens
- **Pro ($12/month)**: Unlimited projects, AI features
- **Business ($39/month)**: Team seats, priority support

## Best For

Non-designers, founders, and product managers who need to quickly prototype app ideas, create wireframes, and communicate design concepts without hiring a designer.
