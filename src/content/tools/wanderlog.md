Wanderlog is an AI-powered travel planning app that helps you build detailed itineraries, discover restaurants and attractions, and organize everything in one place. Its AI suggests things to do based on your destination and travel style, while the collaborative features let you plan trips with friends and family in real time.

## Key Features

- AI itinerary generation from destination and dates
- Restaurant and attraction recommendations
- Interactive map with all trip stops
- Collaborative planning with travel companions
- Flight and hotel booking integration
- Offline access for on-the-go use

## Pricing

- **Free**: Unlimited trips, core features
- **Pro ($35.99/year)**: Offline maps, ad-free, premium features

## Best For

Travelers who want to plan detailed, organized trips with AI-suggested itineraries — and coordinate with travel companions in a shared, collaborative planning space.
