Descript revolutionizes video editing by letting you edit video like a document — delete words from the transcript and the video cuts automatically. Its AI removes filler words, clones your voice for corrections, generates captions, and creates highlight clips. The fastest way to edit talking-head videos and podcasts.

## Key Features

- Edit video by editing text transcript
- AI filler word removal (um, uh, silence)
- Overdub — voice cloning for corrections
- Auto-generated captions and subtitles
- Screen recording with webcam overlay
- Clip creation for social media

## Pricing

- **Free**: 1 hour transcription/month, watermark
- **Hobbyist ($24/month)**: 10 hours, no watermark
- **Creator ($40/month)**: Unlimited, all AI features

## Best For

Video creators, podcasters, and content teams who want to edit talking-head videos and podcasts as fast as editing text — without traditional timeline-based video editing.
