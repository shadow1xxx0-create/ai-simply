Mailchimp is the world's most popular email marketing platform, now powered by AI. Its Intuit Assist AI can write email subject lines, body copy, and entire campaigns from a brief. AI-powered send time optimization ensures emails land when subscribers are most likely to open them, and predictive segmentation targets the right audience automatically.

## Key Features

- AI email content and subject line generation
- Send time optimization using ML
- Predictive segmentation and audience insights
- A/B testing with AI-recommended variants
- Customer journey builder with automation
- Landing page and form builder

## Pricing

- **Free**: 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month
- **Essentials ($13/month)**: 5,000 emails/month, A/B testing
- **Standard ($20/month)**: 6,000 emails/month, AI features
- **Premium ($350/month)**: Advanced segmentation, multivariate testing

## Best For

Small businesses, creators, and e-commerce brands who want an all-in-one email marketing platform with AI features and the industry's largest template and integration ecosystem.
