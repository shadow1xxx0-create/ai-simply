Youper is an AI mental health app that helps you understand and manage emotions through guided CBT exercises, mood tracking, and journaling. The AI learns your emotional patterns over time and provides personalized interventions. Clinically validated and developed with mental health professionals, Youper is available 24/7 between therapy sessions.

## Key Features

- CBT-based conversational AI therapy
- Daily mood and emotion tracking
- Personalized emotional insights
- Mindfulness and breathing exercises
- Symptom tracking for anxiety and depression
- Export reports to share with therapists

## Pricing

- **Free**: Basic mood tracking, limited conversations
- **Premium ($9.99/month)**: Unlimited conversations, full features

## Best For

People managing anxiety, stress, or mild depression who want daily mental health support between therapy sessions — or as a first step toward professional care.
