Klaviyo is the leading email and SMS marketing platform for e-commerce brands. Its AI capabilities include predictive customer lifetime value, churn risk scoring, product recommendations, and AI-generated subject lines. Deep Shopify and WooCommerce integration makes it the default choice for serious D2C brands.

## Key Features

- Predictive CLV and churn risk scoring
- AI product recommendation engine
- AI subject line and content suggestions
- Automated flows (abandoned cart, post-purchase)
- Deep Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce integrations
- SMS and MMS campaigns alongside email

## Pricing

- **Free**: 500 emails/month, 150 SMS credits
- **Email (from $20/month)**: Scales by contact count
- **Email + SMS**: Combined pricing by usage

## Best For

E-commerce brands on Shopify and WooCommerce who want sophisticated email automation, predictive AI, and deep revenue attribution tied to their store data.
