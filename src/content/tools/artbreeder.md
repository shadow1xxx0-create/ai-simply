Artbreeder takes a unique approach to AI art — instead of generating from text prompts, you blend existing images together using sliders to control genes like facial features, colors, and styles. It's especially popular for creating portraits, fantasy characters, album covers, and world-building visuals.

## Key Features

- Image blending with genetic-style sliders
- Portraits, landscapes, and abstract generators
- Splicer — mix any two images together
- Outpainter for extending images
- Large library of community-created images
- Collager for text-to-image compositing

## Pricing

- **Free**: 3 images/month, limited features
- **Starter ($8.99/month)**: 100 credits, commercial use
- **Advanced ($18.99/month)**: 275 credits, priority

## Best For

Game designers, worldbuilders, character creators, and artists who want to evolve and blend AI images rather than generate purely from text prompts.
