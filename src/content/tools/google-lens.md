Google Lens uses AI to understand and search what's in any image. Point your camera at an object and Lens identifies it, finds similar products to buy, reads text (OCR), translates signs in real time, identifies plants and animals, and finds restaurants based on photos. Deeply integrated into Google Search and Android.

## Key Features

- Object identification and visual search
- Text extraction (OCR) from any image
- Real-time translation of text in photos
- Plant, animal, and landmark recognition
- Product search and price comparison
- Works on Android, iOS, and in Google Search

## Pricing

- **Free**: Completely free, built into Google products

## Best For

Anyone who wants to instantly identify objects, extract text from images, translate signs, or find products by photo — especially useful when traveling.
