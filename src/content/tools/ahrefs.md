Ahrefs is the industry standard for SEO research, trusted by over 500,000 SEO professionals. It has the largest backlink database in the industry and powerful keyword research tools. AI features include an AI content grader, automated site audit prioritization, and AI-generated SEO recommendations.

## Key Features

- Backlink database with 30+ trillion links
- Keywords Explorer with 28 billion keywords
- Site Audit for technical SEO issues
- Content Explorer for content ideation
- Rank Tracker for position monitoring
- AI Content Grader and optimization tips

## Pricing

- **Lite ($129/month)**: 5 projects, basic features
- **Standard ($249/month)**: Unlimited history, 20 projects
- **Advanced ($449/month)**: Full features, API access
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing, SSO

## Best For

SEO professionals, digital agencies, and in-house marketing teams who need deep competitive intelligence, backlink analysis, and comprehensive keyword research data.
