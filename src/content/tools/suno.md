Suno is the most popular AI music generator — you describe a song in a few words and it produces a complete track with vocals, instruments, and lyrics. Covers every genre from pop and hip-hop to classical and jazz. The quality is remarkably good, making it accessible to anyone who wants to create original music.

## Key Features

- Complete song generation (vocals + instruments + lyrics)
- Any genre, mood, and style
- Custom mode for writing your own lyrics
- Covers — reimagine existing songs in new styles
- Extend and edit generated songs
- Commercial use rights (paid plans)

## Pricing

- **Free**: 50 credits/day (~10 songs)
- **Pro ($8/month)**: 2,500 credits/month, commercial use
- **Premier ($24/month)**: 10,000 credits/month

## Best For

Musicians, content creators, game developers, and anyone who wants to create original songs or background music without musical training.
