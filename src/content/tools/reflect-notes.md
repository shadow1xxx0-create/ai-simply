Reflect is an AI-first note-taking app designed to be your thinking partner. It connects ideas across all your notes using AI, surfaces related content as you write, and integrates with your calendar to automatically create meeting notes.

## Key Features

- AI Writing Assistant — continue thoughts, summarize, extract action items
- Networked notes with automatic backlinks
- Calendar integration for meeting notes
- Daily notes with automatic date-linking
- Voice memos transcribed and added to notes
- End-to-end encrypted

## Pricing

- **Personal ($10/month)**: Unlimited notes, AI features, all integrations
- **Team ($20/user/month)**: Shared notes, collaboration

## Best For

Knowledge workers, founders, and consultants who think through writing and want AI to help connect the dots across all their notes and meetings.
