Scite is unique in the research tool space — it doesn't just show that a paper was cited, it shows whether the citing paper supports, contrasts, or just mentions the claim. This helps researchers quickly assess the credibility and replication status of scientific findings without reading every paper.

## Key Features

- Smart Citations — supporting vs. contrasting citations
- 1.2 billion citation statements indexed
- Research Assistant for literature synthesis
- Reference checks to find retracted papers
- Journal impact with smart citation metrics
- Zotero integration

## Pricing

- **Free**: Limited searches and citations
- **Individual ($20/month)**: Unlimited searches, full features
- **Team ($15/user/month)**: Shared dashboards

## Best For

Researchers, scientists, and academics who want to assess the reliability and replication status of published findings — not just find papers, but understand how well-supported they are.
