Domo is a cloud-based business intelligence platform that connects to 1,000+ data sources and centralizes everything into AI-powered dashboards and alerts. Its AI layer, Domo.AI, surfaces anomalies, predicts trends, and answers business questions in natural language — no data analyst required for day-to-day insights.

## Key Features

- 1,000+ native data connectors
- AI-powered anomaly detection and forecasting
- Natural language queries via Domo.AI
- Mobile-first dashboard experience
- Automated alerts and scheduled reports
- App Studio for building custom data apps

- **Standard**: Contact sales for pricing
- **Enterprise**: Custom volume and feature tiers

## Best For

Mid-market and enterprise teams who need a single platform to connect disparate data sources, share insights across the organization, and act on AI-driven recommendations.
