Adobe Podcast's AI tools transform amateur recordings into studio-quality audio. Enhance Speech removes background noise and improves voice clarity from any recording — even from a laptop microphone. Mic Check analyzes your microphone setup in real time and gives suggestions. Part of Adobe's broader Creative Cloud ecosystem.

## Key Features

- Enhance Speech — one-click noise removal and voice enhancement
- Mic Check — real-time microphone quality analysis
- Transcript-based podcast editing
- AI-generated summaries and chapter markers
- Web-based (no software download needed)
- Adobe Creative Cloud integration

## Pricing

- **Free**: 1 hour of enhanced audio/month
- **Included with Creative Cloud**: Full access

## Best For

Podcasters, YouTubers, remote workers, and anyone who needs to clean up audio quality without expensive equipment or complex audio editing software.
