Slack AI adds powerful AI capabilities directly into Slack. Channel Recaps summarize what happened while you were away. Thread Summaries compress long discussions into bullet points. AI Search understands natural language questions and searches your entire Slack history. Workflow Builder now uses AI to automate tasks.

## Key Features

- Channel Recaps — daily/weekly AI summaries
- Thread Summaries for long discussions
- AI Search across all messages, files, and channels
- Workflow Builder with AI automation
- Huddle transcripts and summaries
- Integration with connected apps (Salesforce, Jira)

## Pricing

- **AI add-on ($10/user/month)**: Requires paid Slack plan
- **Pro ($8.75/user/month)**: Base Slack plan
- **Business+ ($15/user/month)**: Advanced features
- **Enterprise Grid**: Custom pricing

## Best For

Companies already using Slack who want to eliminate information overload, help distributed teams stay caught up, and make their Slack history searchable and useful.
