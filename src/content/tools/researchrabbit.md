ResearchRabbit is a free AI-powered research discovery tool that maps academic papers as visual networks. Add papers you know, and it finds related work, traces citation paths, and alerts you when new relevant papers are published. Think of it as Spotify recommendations but for academic research.

## Key Features

- Visual citation network mapping
- AI-powered paper recommendations
- Email alerts for new related publications
- Zotero integration for reference management
- Collaborative collections with teammates
- Completely free forever

## Pricing

- **Free**: Full access, no limitations

## Best For

Researchers, PhD students, and academics who want to discover related work visually, stay current on new publications, and map entire research fields efficiently.
