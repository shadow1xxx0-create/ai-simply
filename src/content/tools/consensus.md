Consensus is an AI search engine built specifically for scientific literature. Ask any research question and it searches 200 million+ peer-reviewed papers to find studies that answer it. Consensus Meter shows what percentage of studies agree or disagree with your question. GPT-4-powered summaries synthesize findings across multiple papers.

## Key Features

- Searches 200 million+ peer-reviewed papers
- Consensus Meter — shows scientific consensus
- GPT-4 synthesized summaries
- Best practices extraction from literature
- Study Snapshot for quick paper summaries
- Filters by study design, journal, year

## Pricing

- **Free**: 20 searches/month, basic features
- **Premium ($9.99/month)**: Unlimited searches, advanced filters
- **Enterprise**: API, team access

## Best For

Researchers, healthcare professionals, evidence-based practitioners, and students who need to quickly assess what the scientific literature says on specific topics.
