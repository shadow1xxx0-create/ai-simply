DoNotPay is an AI-powered consumer rights app that automates legal tasks anyone might face — appealing parking tickets, disputing bank charges, getting refunds from airlines, canceling trial subscriptions, and even generating legal demand letters. It's the easiest way to fight back against large companies.

## Key Features

- Parking ticket appeals (60%+ success rate)
- Automatic subscription cancellation
- Chargeback and refund letter generator
- Small claims court filing assistance
- Spam call and mail blocking
- Credit card interest rate negotiation

## Pricing

- **Monthly ($36/month)**: Full access to all features

## Best For

Consumers who want to fight unfair charges, appeal fines, cancel unwanted subscriptions, and assert their legal rights without hiring a lawyer or spending hours on the phone.
