Notion is the all-in-one workspace where teams write docs, manage projects, and store knowledge — now with AI built in. Notion AI can summarize meeting notes, draft SOPs, fill in databases, answer questions about your company wiki, and automate repetitive writing tasks. It becomes the AI brain of your entire organization's knowledge base.

## Key Features

- AI writing, editing, and summarization on any page
- Q&A — ask questions across your entire workspace
- Database automation with AI-filled properties
- Meeting notes to action items automatically
- Shared team wikis and knowledge bases
- Integrates with Slack, GitHub, Figma, and more

## Pricing

- **Free**: Limited blocks, basic AI
- **Plus ($10/user/month)**: Unlimited pages, AI add-on available
- **Business ($18/user/month)**: Advanced permissions, AI included
- **Enterprise**: SSO, audit logs, custom pricing

## Best For

Teams who want a single workspace for documents, projects, and company knowledge — with AI that understands your entire organization's context and helps automate writing and information management.
