Taskade combines project management, docs, mind maps, and AI agents in one workspace. The AI can generate complete project plans from a description, create custom AI agents for specific tasks, automate recurring workflows, and answer questions about your project data. Available across web, desktop, and mobile.

## Key Features

- AI project and task generation from descriptions
- Custom AI agents for any workflow
- Mind maps, kanban boards, lists, and calendars
- Real-time team collaboration
- AI meeting assistant with summaries
- Templates for 1,000+ use cases

## Pricing

- **Free**: Unlimited projects, limited AI credits
- **Plus ($19/month)**: AI agents, unlimited credits
- **Pro ($49/month)**: Advanced AI, custom agents
- **Business ($99/month)**: Team workspace, analytics

## Best For

Remote teams, freelancers, and solopreneurs who want an all-in-one workspace combining task management, docs, and AI agents without switching between multiple tools.
