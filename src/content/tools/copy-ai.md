Copy.ai is a popular AI writing tool focused on marketing and sales copy. It offers hundreds of templates for ads, email campaigns, product descriptions, social posts, and sales sequences. The GTM AI Platform is their enterprise offering that connects AI to your entire go-to-market workflow.

## Key Features

- 90+ copywriting templates
- Blog Wizard for long-form content
- Email and social post generators
- Sales copy and cold outreach templates
- Brand voice customization
- Workflow automation (GTM AI)

## Pricing

- **Free**: 2,000 words/month, 90+ tools
- **Pro ($36/month)**: Unlimited words, priority support
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing, API access

## Best For

Marketing teams, startups, freelance copywriters, and sales teams who need to produce high-converting copy quickly.
