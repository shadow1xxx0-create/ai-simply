Frase solves the research problem in content marketing. Before you write a single word, it analyzes the top 20 ranking pages for your target keyword — extracting headings, questions people ask, key statistics, and content structure. Then you write against that research with real-time SEO scoring.

## Key Features

- SERP analysis — scrapes and summarizes top-ranking content for any keyword
- AI content briefs with recommended headings and word count
- AI writer for drafting sections with one click
- SEO optimization score updates in real time as you write
- People Also Ask integration for FAQ sections
- Team collaboration on content briefs

## Pricing

- **Solo ($15/month)**: 4 articles/month, full features
- **Basic ($45/month)**: 30 articles/month
- **Team ($115/month)**: Unlimited articles, team access
- **Pro Add-on (+$35/month)**: Unlimited AI writing

## Best For

SEO content writers, content marketing managers, and agencies who want their blog posts to rank — not just be published.
