Winston AI is an AI content detection tool designed specifically for publishers and educators who need high-confidence detection of AI-generated text. It claims 99.98% accuracy on ChatGPT and Claude content, provides a human-generated probability score, and offers a Chrome extension for quick in-browser checks.

## Key Features

- 99.98% claimed accuracy on major AI models
- Human-generated probability score (0-100%)
- Chrome extension for quick checks
- Plagiarism detection add-on
- OCR for scanning images and PDFs
- Team accounts for agencies

## Pricing

- **Free**: 2,000 words/month
- **Essential ($18/month)**: 80,000 words/month
- **Advanced ($29/month)**: 200,000 words/month

## Best For

Publishers, content agencies, educators, and HR teams who need a reliable AI detector to verify that submitted content is human-written before publishing or accepting it.
