Monday.com's AI assistant is embedded across their work management platform. It can generate project plans from descriptions, summarize board updates, write automation formulas in natural language, create email drafts, and identify at-risk tasks before they become problems. Available across all Monday products.

## Key Features

- AI project plan generation from a description
- Automated board summaries and status updates
- Natural language automation builder
- AI email and update drafts
- Risk identification for projects
- Formula column AI for complex calculations

## Pricing

- **Free**: 2 seats, core features (no AI)
- **Basic ($12/seat/month)**: Unlimited items
- **Standard ($14/seat/month)**: AI features included
- **Pro ($24/seat/month)**: Advanced automation
- **Enterprise**: SSO, advanced security

## Best For

Project managers, operations teams, and agencies who need a flexible work management platform with AI that automates status reporting and workflow setup.
