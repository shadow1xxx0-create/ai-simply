Ideogram is an AI image generator known for its exceptional ability to render readable text within images — a major weakness of most AI image tools. It generates photorealistic images, illustrations, and designs with accurate typography, making it the go-to tool for creating AI images that include legible words, logos, and signs.

## Key Features

- Best-in-class text rendering in generated images
- Photorealistic and illustration styles
- Magic Prompt for automatic prompt enhancement
- Remix and variation generation
- Aspect ratio control for all platforms
- Free tier with generous daily generations

## Pricing

- **Free**: 10 slow generations/day
- **Basic ($7/month)**: 400 priority generations/month
- **Plus ($16/month)**: 1,000 priority generations/month

## Best For

Designers, marketers, and creators who need AI images with accurate readable text — for social graphics, mockups, posters, and any image where words need to appear correctly.
