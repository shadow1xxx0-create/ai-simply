FeedHive uses AI to help you write, schedule, and predict the performance of social media posts before you publish. Its Performance Prediction feature shows which posts are likely to go viral. Content recycling automatically reposts your best-performing content. Strong on Twitter/X and LinkedIn.

## Key Features

- AI Performance Prediction before publishing
- Content recycling for evergreen posts
- AI writing assistant with tone control
- Conditional posting (auto-repost if post performs well)
- Hashtag and @mention manager
- Multi-account team collaboration
