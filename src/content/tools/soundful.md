Soundful is an AI music platform designed specifically for content creators who need royalty-free background music. Pick a genre template, adjust tempo and key, and generate unique tracks instantly. Every generated track is commercially licensed, making it safe for YouTube, social media, and brand videos without copyright claims.

## Key Features

- 50+ genre templates (lo-fi, cinematic, pop, EDM, etc.)
- Adjust tempo, key, and energy level
- Commercially licensed, royalty-free tracks
- High-quality WAV and MP3 downloads
- No copyright claims on YouTube or social media
- Unlimited track generation on paid plans

## Pricing

- **Free**: 10 downloads/month, standard quality
- **Creator ($9.99/month)**: 30 high-quality downloads/month
- **Unlimited ($49.99/month)**: Unlimited downloads, stems included

YouTubers, video editors, social media creators, and marketers who need reliable, royalty-free background music without copyright headaches.
