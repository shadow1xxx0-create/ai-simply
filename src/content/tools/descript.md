Descript reimagines audio and video editing — you edit the transcript like a document and the media follows. Its AI transcription is near-perfect, and Overdub lets you clone your own voice to fix audio mistakes by typing new words. Loved by podcasters, YouTubers, and video marketers.

## Key Features

- Edit audio/video by editing the transcript
- Overdub — voice cloning to fix mistakes
- Remove filler words ("um", "uh") automatically
- Screen recording built in
- AI-generated show notes and summaries
- Multitrack audio editing

## Pricing

- **Free**: 1 hour transcription/month
- **Hobbyist ($24/month)**: 10 hours/month, Overdub
- **Creator ($40/month)**: 30 hours, 4K export
- **Enterprise**: Unlimited, team features

## Best For

Podcasters, YouTubers, and video content creators who want a faster, more intuitive way to edit long-form audio and video content.
