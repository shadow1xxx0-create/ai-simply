Casetext is an AI-powered legal research platform used by thousands of law firms. Its CoCounsel AI assistant — built on GPT-4 — can research case law, draft legal documents, review contracts, and answer complex legal questions in minutes. Acquired by Thomson Reuters, it integrates with Westlaw for comprehensive legal research coverage.

## Key Features

- CoCounsel AI for legal research and drafting
- Case law search across all US jurisdictions
- Contract review and clause identification
- Deposition preparation assistance
- Brief analysis and summarization
- Integration with Thomson Reuters / Westlaw

## Pricing

- **CoCounsel**: From $100/month per user
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for law firms
- **Thomson Reuters subscribers**: Bundled access available

## Best For

Attorneys, law firms, and legal departments who need to accelerate research, contract review, and document drafting with a court-tested AI legal assistant.
