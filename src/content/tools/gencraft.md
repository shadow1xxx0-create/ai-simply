Gencraft is an AI art generation app focused on mobile users and creative exploration. It offers dozens of artistic styles — from oil painting to anime to photorealism — and generates stunning visuals from text prompts in seconds. Popular among social media creators who want unique AI artwork on the go.

## Key Features

- 50+ artistic style presets
- Text-to-image generation in seconds
- Image-to-image style transfer
- Video generation from images
- Mobile-first design (iOS and Android)
- Community gallery for inspiration

## Pricing

- **Free**: 10 generations/day
- **Pro ($9.99/month)**: Unlimited generations, all styles
- **Ultimate ($19.99/month)**: Priority generation, HD output

## Best For

Mobile users, social media creators, and casual artists who want to create AI artwork quickly in various styles — without needing a desktop or technical setup.
