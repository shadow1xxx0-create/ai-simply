HubSpot is an all-in-one CRM platform with AI woven throughout its Sales, Marketing, Service, and Content hubs. HubSpot AI assists with writing emails, scoring leads, forecasting revenue, summarizing calls, and generating reports — all inside the same platform your team already uses to manage customers.

## Key Features

- AI email writing and personalization
- Predictive lead scoring
- ChatSpot — AI assistant for CRM queries
- Call summarization and coaching
- AI-powered content generation
- Revenue forecasting and pipeline insights

## Pricing

- **Free**: Basic CRM, limited AI features
- **Starter ($15/month)**: Core hubs with AI assistance
- **Professional ($800/month)**: Full automation and AI
- **Enterprise ($3,600/month)**: Advanced AI and reporting

## Best For

Sales teams, marketers, and customer service teams who want AI assistance built into every part of their CRM without stitching together separate tools.
