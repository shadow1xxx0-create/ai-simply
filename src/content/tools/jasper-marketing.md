Jasper is an AI marketing platform built for marketing teams who need to produce high volumes of on-brand content. It learns your brand voice, product details, and audience — then generates campaigns, ad copy, emails, landing pages, and social posts that sound like your team wrote them. Used by marketing teams at major brands worldwide.

## Key Features

- Brand voice training and enforcement
- Campaign brief to full content generation
- Ad copy for Google, Meta, and LinkedIn
- Email sequences and landing page copy
- Marketing template library (50+ templates)
- Team collaboration and approval workflows

## Pricing

- **Creator ($49/month)**: 1 user, unlimited words
- **Teams ($125/month)**: 3 users, brand voice, campaigns
- **Business**: Custom pricing for large teams

## Best For

Marketing teams and growth-focused businesses who need to produce large volumes of consistent, on-brand content across channels without burning out their writers.
