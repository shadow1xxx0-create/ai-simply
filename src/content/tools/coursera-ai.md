Coursera offers 7,000+ courses from 300+ top universities (Stanford, Google, IBM, Microsoft) and has embedded AI throughout its learning experience. Coursera Coach provides personalized recommendations, answers questions about course material, and helps learners stay on track. Generative AI courses are their fastest-growing category.

## Key Features

- AI-personalized course recommendations
- Coursera Coach for instant learning support
- 7,000+ courses from top universities
- Professional certificates recognized by employers
- Hands-on projects and graded assignments
- Learn at your own pace with lifetime access

## Pricing

- **Free**: Audit most courses (no certificates)
- **Coursera Plus ($59/month or $399/year)**: 7,000+ courses with certificates
- **Enterprise**: Custom team learning

## Best For

Professionals looking to upskill, career changers pursuing new fields, and anyone who wants university-level education with recognized certificates from top institutions.
