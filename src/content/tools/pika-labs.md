Pika is a user-friendly AI video generation platform that creates short, high-quality video clips from text prompts, images, or existing videos. Known for its smooth, cinematic motion and creative styles, Pika makes video generation accessible to everyone — no technical skills required.

## Key Features

- Text to video generation
- Image to video animation
- Modify region — edit specific parts of a video
- Lip sync for talking video
- Multiple cinematic style options
- Sound effects generation

## Pricing

- **Free**: 250 credits/month
- **Basic ($8/month)**: 700 credits/month
- **Standard ($28/month)**: 2,000 credits/month
- **Unlimited ($78/month)**: Unlimited standard generations

## Best For

Content creators, social media managers, and anyone who wants to create short, eye-catching video content from images or text without complex tools.
