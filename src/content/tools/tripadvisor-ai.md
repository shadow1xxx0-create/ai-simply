Tripadvisor's AI-powered trip planning tool generates personalized day-by-day travel itineraries based on your destination, interests, budget, and travel style. Built on top of Tripadvisor's massive database of 1 billion+ reviews, it recommends hotels, restaurants, and activities that are genuinely well-reviewed, not just AI hallucinations.

## Key Features

- Day-by-day itinerary generation
- Personalized to interests and travel style
- Pulls from 1B+ real traveler reviews
- Hotels and restaurants with booking links
- Save and share itineraries
- Adjust and regenerate specific days

## Pricing

- **Free**: Fully free to use

## Best For

Travelers who want a starting point for trip planning without spending hours researching — especially useful for new destinations or time-constrained planning.
