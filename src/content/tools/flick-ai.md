Flick is an AI-powered social media tool focused on content creation and hashtag strategy. Its AI Social Media Assistant generates on-brand captions, suggests relevant hashtags, brainstorms content ideas, and repurposes content across platforms. Built specifically for creators and small businesses who want to grow on social without spending hours writing posts.

## Key Features

- AI caption writing in your brand voice
- Hashtag research and performance tracking
- Content idea generation by niche and platform
- Content repurposing across Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn
- Scheduling and publishing calendar
- Analytics to see what content performs best

## Pricing

- **Solo ($14/month)**: 1 user, AI assistant, 4 profiles
- **Pro ($30/month)**: 2 users, analytics, 8 profiles
- **Agency ($85/month)**: 5 users, 20 profiles

## Best For

Creators, coaches, and small businesses who want to post consistently on social media without spending hours writing captions — using AI to generate on-brand content in minutes.
