Anki is the most powerful flashcard app available, using a proven spaced repetition algorithm to show you cards right before you forget them. Supports text, images, audio, and LaTeX. Used by medical students, language learners, and anyone who needs to memorize large amounts of information.

## Key Features
- Spaced repetition algorithm (SM-2)
- Supports images, audio, and LaTeX
- Sync across desktop and mobile
- Thousands of shared community decks
- Free on all platforms (iOS app is paid)
