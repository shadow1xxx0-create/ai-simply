Houzz is the world's largest home design platform with 70 million+ photos and a marketplace of 20 million+ products. The AI-powered View in My Room feature lets you see exactly how furniture and decor will look in your actual space using AR. Houzz Pro is a full business management platform for interior designers and contractors.

## Key Features

- View in My Room AR for product visualization
- 70 million+ home design photo inspiration
- 20 million+ shoppable products
- Sketch to design AI concept rendering
- Houzz Pro for design professionals
- Find and hire local contractors

## Pricing

- **Free**: Browse, save ideas, shop, AR tool
- **Houzz Pro (from $85/month)**: Business tools for professionals

## Best For

Homeowners looking for design inspiration and wanting to visualize products in their space, and interior design professionals managing client projects.
