Zendesk AI (powered by OpenAI) automatically triages tickets, suggests responses to agents, and resolves common issues without human involvement. The AI agent handles up to 80% of routine customer inquiries across chat, email, and social — while seamlessly escalating complex issues to human agents with full context.

## Key Features

- AI agents that resolve tickets autonomously
- Intelligent triage and routing
- Agent Copilot for response suggestions
- Automatic ticket summarization
- Intent and sentiment detection
- 1,000+ integrations

## Pricing

- **Suite Team ($69/agent/month)**: Core AI features
- **Suite Growth ($115/agent/month)**: Advanced automation
- **Suite Professional ($149/agent/month)**: Full AI suite
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing

## Best For

Mid-to-large businesses with high support ticket volume who need to automate repetitive customer service tasks and improve agent productivity without sacrificing quality.
