LOVO AI combines AI voiceover generation with a built-in video editor — making it a one-stop shop for creating narrated video content. With 500+ voices in 100 languages and an integrated timeline editor, you can generate a voiceover and sync it to your video without switching between tools. Popular for e-learning, marketing videos, and explainers.

## Key Features

- 500+ AI voices in 100 languages
- Integrated video editor with timeline
- Voice cloning from 10 seconds of audio
- Granular voice control (pitch, speed, emphasis)
- Background music and sound effects library
- Screen recorder with AI narration

E-learning developers, marketers, and video creators who want to produce narrated videos with professional AI voices — with editing and voiceover in one integrated tool.
