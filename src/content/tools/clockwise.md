Clockwise is an AI-powered calendar tool that automatically rearranges your schedule to create longer blocks of uninterrupted focus time. It moves flexible meetings, coordinates team schedules, and protects your deep work hours — all without you lifting a finger. Integrates directly with Google Calendar.

## Key Features

- Automatic focus time protection
- Smart meeting rescheduling
- Team schedule coordination
- Slack status sync with calendar
- Meeting analytics and time audit
- Flexible meeting detection and optimization

## Pricing

- **Free**: Basic scheduling, 1 calendar
- **Teams ($6.75/user/month)**: Full AI optimization, team features
- **Business ($11.50/user/month)**: Advanced analytics, admin controls

## Best For

Knowledge workers and teams who want AI to automatically protect their deep work time and reduce calendar chaos without manual scheduling effort.
