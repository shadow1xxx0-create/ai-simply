Socratic is a free AI homework help app by Google. Take a photo of any question — math, science, history, English — and it instantly provides step-by-step explanations, visual aids, and links to relevant learning resources. Trusted by millions of students worldwide for understanding concepts, not just getting answers.

## Key Features

- Photo-based question recognition
- Step-by-step explanations across all subjects
- Math problem solving with shown work
- Science, history, English, and more
- Visual learning aids and diagrams
- Links to vetted educational resources

## Pricing

- **Free**: Full access, no limitations

## Best For

Middle school and high school students who need help understanding homework problems — with step-by-step explanations that teach the concept, not just give the answer.
