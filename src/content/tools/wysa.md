Wysa is an AI mental health companion that uses evidence-based Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) techniques to help users manage anxiety, stress, depression, and sleep issues. Available 24/7, it provides a judgment-free space to talk through emotions and practice coping skills — with the option to escalate to a human therapist when needed.

## Key Features

- CBT and DBT-based exercises and techniques
- Anonymous and judgment-free conversation
- Mood tracking and emotional patterns
- Sleep improvement and stress management tools
- Crisis support with escalation to human coaches
- HIPAA-compliant and clinically validated

## Pricing

- **Free**: Basic AI chat and exercises
- **Premium ($29.99/month)**: Full exercise library, therapist access
- **Enterprise**: Employee mental health programs

## Best For

People dealing with everyday stress, anxiety, or low mood who want accessible, evidence-based mental health support — available any time, without the cost or waitlist of traditional therapy.
