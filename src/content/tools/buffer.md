Buffer is a social media management platform with AI tools for content generation, scheduling, and analytics. The AI assistant can generate post ideas, write captions, repurpose content across platforms, and analyze which posts perform best. Used by 140,000+ businesses and creators.

## Key Features

- Schedule posts across 8+ platforms
- AI post idea and caption generator
- Content repurposing across channels
- Analytics and best-time-to-post insights
- Instagram Stories and Reels scheduling
- Team collaboration and approval workflows

## Pricing

- **Free**: 3 channels, 10 scheduled posts
- **Essentials ($6/month/channel)**: Unlimited scheduling, analytics
- **Team ($12/month/channel)**: Collaboration tools

## Best For

Small businesses, creators, freelancers, and social media managers who need a simple, affordable tool to manage and schedule content across platforms.
