Grammarly is the most widely used AI writing assistant, installed by over 30 million people. It checks grammar, spelling, punctuation, style, and tone in real time — and with Grammarly AI, it can also rewrite sentences, adjust your communication style, and generate full drafts. Works in browsers, Word, Google Docs, and desktop apps.

## Key Features

- Real-time grammar, spelling, and punctuation correction
- Tone and clarity suggestions
- Full-sentence rewrites and paraphrasing
- AI-generated drafts from prompts
- Plagiarism detection (Premium)
- Works in 500,000+ apps and websites

## Pricing

- **Free**: Basic grammar and spelling
- **Premium ($12/month)**: Style, clarity, tone, rewrites
- **Business ($15/user/month)**: Team features, style guides

## Best For

Everyone who writes professionally — students, marketers, executives, and non-native English speakers who want polished, error-free writing.
