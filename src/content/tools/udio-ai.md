Udio generates complete, high-quality songs with real vocals from text prompts — covering any genre from jazz to death metal. Launched by ex-Google DeepMind researchers, Udio produces some of the most realistic AI music available, with natural-sounding vocals that are hard to distinguish from human singers. You can extend songs, create variations, and download tracks for commercial use.

## Key Features

- Full songs with AI vocals from text descriptions
- Any genre — pop, rock, jazz, classical, hip-hop, and more
- Song extension and variation generation
- Download in high-quality audio formats
- Remix and edit generated songs
- Community feed to explore others' creations

Musicians, content creators, and anyone who wants to generate complete, professional-sounding songs with real vocals — across any genre — without any music production skills.
