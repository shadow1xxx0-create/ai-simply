Tableau is the leading business intelligence and data visualization platform, now with deep AI integration through Tableau AI (Einstein Copilot). Ask questions about your data in natural language, get AI-generated insights, create visualizations automatically, and share interactive dashboards across your organization.

## Key Features

- Natural language data queries
- AI-generated chart recommendations
- Einstein Copilot for automated insights
- Drag-and-drop dashboard builder
- Salesforce CRM data integration
- Real-time data connections to 100+ sources

## Pricing

- **Viewer ($15/user/month)**: View and interact with dashboards
- **Explorer ($42/user/month)**: Build visualizations
- **Creator ($75/user/month)**: Full authoring, data prep

## Best For

Business analysts, data teams, and executives at mid-to-large companies who need to make data-driven decisions through interactive, AI-enhanced dashboards.
