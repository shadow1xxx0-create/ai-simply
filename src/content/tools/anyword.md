Anyword is the AI writing tool that predicts how well your copy will perform before you publish it. Every headline, ad, and email it generates comes with a Predictive Performance Score — a number trained on billions of marketing data points that estimates conversion likelihood. Write 10 subject lines and pick the one most likely to get opened.

## Key Features

- Predictive Performance Score for every piece of copy
- 200+ copywriting templates (ads, emails, landing pages, social)
- Brand Voice training from existing content
- Data-Driven Editor — analyze and score existing copy
- Blog wizard for SEO articles
- Team collaboration and approval workflows

## Pricing

- **Starter ($49/month)**: 1 seat, unlimited words, performance scoring
- **Data-Driven ($99/month)**: Custom AI models, advanced scoring
- **Business ($499/month)**: Multiple brand voices, team features
- **Enterprise**: Custom

## Best For

Performance marketers, growth teams, and agencies who need copy that converts — not just copy that reads well.
