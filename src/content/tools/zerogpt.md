ZeroGPT is one of the most widely used free AI detection tools, capable of identifying text generated by ChatGPT, GPT-4, Gemini, and other AI models. It provides an AI percentage score and highlights specific sentences flagged as AI-generated. With no account required for basic use, it's the go-to quick check for students, teachers, and content reviewers.

## Key Features

- Detects ChatGPT, GPT-4, Gemini, and other AI models
- AI percentage score with confidence level
- Sentence-level highlighting of AI-generated text
- No account required for basic checks
- Batch file upload for documents
- API access for automated workflows

## Pricing

- **Free**: Up to 15,000 characters per check
- **Premium ($9.99/month)**: Higher limits, batch processing, API
- **Enterprise**: Custom volume and integrations

## Best For

Teachers, students, content editors, and HR teams who need a quick, free way to check whether submitted text was written by AI — without any technical setup.
