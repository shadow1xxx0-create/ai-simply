Adobe Firefly is Adobe's family of AI image generation models, designed specifically for commercial use. Unlike competitors, Firefly is trained only on licensed Adobe Stock images and public domain content — so everything you generate is safe to use commercially. It's deeply integrated into Photoshop, Illustrator, and Express.

## Key Features

- Commercially safe outputs (licensed training data)
- Generative Fill in Photoshop
- Text to Image and Generative Expand
- Consistent style matching with Style Reference
- Deep Creative Cloud integration
- Structure and Style references for control

## Pricing

- **Free**: 25 generative credits/month
- **Creative Cloud ($60/month)**: 1,000+ credits with full CC suite
- **Firefly Premium ($9.99/month)**: Standalone credits

## Best For

Professional designers, marketers, and agencies who need commercially safe AI images and seamless Adobe workflow integration.
