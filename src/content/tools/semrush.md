Semrush is the industry-leading SEO and digital marketing toolkit, used by over 10 million marketers. ## Key Features

- Keyword research with 26 billion keywords
- Site audit for technical SEO issues
- ContentShake AI writer for SEO content
- Competitor traffic and backlink analysis
- PPC keyword tool and ad research
- Social media management and analytics
