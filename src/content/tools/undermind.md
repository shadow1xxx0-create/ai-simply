Undermind is an AI research agent that conducts deep academic searches and produces comprehensive research reports. Unlike simple paper finders, Undermind reads papers, follows citation chains, evaluates relevance, and synthesizes findings into a structured report — like having a research assistant who can read 500 papers overnight.

## Key Features

- Deep autonomous literature search
- Citation chain following and evaluation
- Structured research report generation
- Source quality assessment
- Covers science, medicine, engineering, and social sciences
- Transparent reasoning about what it found and why

## Pricing

- **Free**: 3 research reports/month
- **Pro ($20/month)**: 20 reports/month, deeper searches

## Best For

Researchers and professionals who need comprehensive literature reviews fast — the AI does the reading and synthesizing, delivering a well-organized report with all sources cited.
