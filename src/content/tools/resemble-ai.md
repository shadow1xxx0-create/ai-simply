Resemble AI is a voice cloning and AI voice generation platform used by developers, media companies, and enterprises. With just a few minutes of audio, it clones any voice with high fidelity — then lets you generate unlimited speech in that voice via API or dashboard. Used for video games, audiobooks, branded voice assistants, and localization.

## Key Features

- High-fidelity voice cloning from short samples
- Real-time voice generation API
- Emotion and tone control
- Neural audio watermarking for authenticity
- Localization in 20+ languages
- Enterprise-grade security and consent tools

## Pricing

- **Free**: 10,000 characters/month
- **Basic ($29/month)**: 100,000 characters, 3 voice clones
- **Pro ($99/month)**: 500,000 characters, unlimited clones
- **Enterprise**: Custom volume and deployment

## Best For

Developers, game studios, and media companies who need high-quality voice cloning and programmatic speech generation for applications, games, audiobooks, or branded voice experiences.
