MyFitnessPal has 200 million+ users and the world's largest food database with 14 million+ foods. The AI provides personalized calorie and macro targets, meal suggestions, food insights, and plans that adapt to your progress. Barcode scanning and restaurant menu tracking make logging fast and accurate.

## Key Features

- 14 million+ food database with barcode scanner
- AI-personalized calorie and macro targets
- Meal and exercise logging
- Restaurant menu tracking
- Progress insights and trend analysis
- Integrates with 50+ fitness apps and wearables

## Pricing

- **Free**: Calorie counting, basic logging, database
- **Premium ($19.99/month)**: Macro goals, food analysis, no ads

## Best For

Anyone tracking their nutrition for weight loss, muscle gain, or general health — particularly people who want the largest food database and wearable device integration.
