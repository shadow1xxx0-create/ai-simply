Polymer Search turns any spreadsheet into an interactive, AI-powered dashboard without any coding or BI expertise. Upload your CSV or connect Google Sheets, and Polymer automatically builds charts, finds patterns, and answers questions about your data. It's designed for teams who need business intelligence but don't have data analysts on staff.

## Key Features

- Automatic dashboard generation from spreadsheets
- AI-powered anomaly and trend detection
- Natural language Q&A on your data
- Embeddable dashboards for clients or stakeholders
- Connect Google Sheets, Airtable, CSV files
- No SQL or coding knowledge required

## Pricing

- **Free**: 1 dataset, basic dashboards
- **Starter ($20/month)**: 5 datasets, full AI features
- **Business ($60/month)**: Unlimited datasets, team sharing

## Best For

Small businesses, freelancers, and operations teams who want to turn their spreadsheet data into professional dashboards and AI-powered insights — without hiring a data analyst.
