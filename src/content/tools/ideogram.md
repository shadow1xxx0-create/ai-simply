Ideogram solves one of AI image generation's biggest weaknesses — text rendering. Where Midjourney and DALL-E struggle with legible words in images, Ideogram excels. It's the go-to tool for creating logos, posters, book covers, social graphics, and any design where readable text matters.

## Key Features

- Best-in-class text rendering within images
- Styles include realistic, design, anime, 3D, and illustration
- Magic Fill for targeted edits
- Prompt magic for automatic prompt enhancement
- Describe — generate prompts from uploaded images
- 10 free generations per day

## Pricing

- **Free**: 10 priority + 25 slow generations/day
- **Basic ($7/month)**: 400 priority generations
- **Plus ($16/month)**: 1,000 priority generations
- **Pro ($48/month)**: 3,000 priority generations

## Best For

Graphic designers, marketers, and creators who need to include readable text in AI-generated images — logos, social media graphics, posters, and typography-heavy designs.
