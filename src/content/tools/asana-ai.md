Asana is one of the world's most widely used project management platforms, and its AI layer — built on top of the existing task and timeline infrastructure — adds intelligent prioritization, automated workflows, and instant status summaries.

## Key Features

- AI Smart Priorities — ranks your tasks by urgency and importance
- AI Project Summary — generates real-time status reports from task data
- AI Workflow Builder — automates repetitive handoffs and notifications
- Goals and OKR tracking with AI progress insights
- Integrations with Slack, Google Workspace, Jira, Salesforce
- Timeline view with AI dependency detection

## Pricing

- **Free**: Up to 10 users, basic task management
- **Starter ($13.49/user/month)**: Timelines, automation, dashboard
- **Advanced ($30.49/user/month)**: AI features, portfolios, workload management
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing, advanced security

## Best For

Mid-size to large teams managing complex projects across departments who want AI to reduce status-meeting overhead and surface what actually needs attention.
