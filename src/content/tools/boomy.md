Boomy lets anyone create original AI-generated songs in seconds, even with zero musical experience. Choose a genre, click "Create Song," and Boomy produces a full track. You can then submit your songs to major streaming platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, and TikTok to earn royalties — making it one of the few AI music tools with a built-in monetization path.

## Key Features

- One-click AI song generation across multiple genres
- Submit tracks to Spotify, Apple Music, TikTok, YouTube Music
- Royalty sharing when your songs stream
- Basic customization of structure and tempo
- Community to discover and share creations
- Millions of songs already created by users

## Pricing

- **Free**: 25 song creations, limited downloads
- **Personal ($9.99/month)**: Unlimited creations, streaming submissions, royalties
- **Pro ($29.99/month)**: Higher royalty split, priority support

## Best For

Aspiring musicians, content creators, and hobbyists who want to create and monetize original music without any music production skills.
