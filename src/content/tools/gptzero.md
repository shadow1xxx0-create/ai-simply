GPTZero is the most widely used AI content detector, built by Princeton student Edward Tian. It analyzes text for "perplexity" and "burstiness" — patterns that distinguish human writing from AI output. Used by educators, publishers, and HR professionals to identify AI-generated content.

## Key Features

- Detects ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other AI models
- Sentence-level highlighting of AI-written sections
- Batch file upload for bulk detection
- API for integration into other tools
- Writing feedback mode
- Plagiarism check add-on

## Pricing

- **Free**: 10,000 characters/check, 3 files/month
- **Essential ($10/month)**: 50,000 chars, 100 files
- **Pro ($16/month)**: 150,000 chars, unlimited files
- **Enterprise**: Custom volume pricing

## Best For

Teachers, professors, publishers, HR teams, and anyone who needs to verify whether submitted text was written by a human or an AI.
