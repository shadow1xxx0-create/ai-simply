Gemini is Google's flagship AI assistant, built on the Gemini 2.0 model family. It's deeply integrated with Google Search, Gmail, Google Docs, and Google Drive, making it a natural fit for anyone already in the Google ecosystem. Gemini Advanced unlocks the most powerful model tier and deep integration with Workspace apps.

## Key Features

- Multimodal: understands text, images, audio, and video
- Deep Google Workspace integration (Gmail, Docs, Sheets)
- Real-time web search built in
- Gemini in Google Search for AI overviews
- 1 million token context window (Advanced)
- Code generation with Google AI Studio

## Pricing

- **Free**: Gemini 2.0 Flash, standard usage
- **Advanced ($19.99/month)**: Gemini 2.0 Ultra, Workspace integration, 2TB storage

## Best For

Google Workspace users, students, and anyone wanting tight integration between AI and their existing Google tools.
