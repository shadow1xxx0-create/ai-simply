Google Maps has integrated AI throughout its navigation and discovery experience. Immersive View uses AI to create photorealistic 3D previews of routes and destinations. AI-powered search understands natural language queries ("coffee shop with outdoor seating near me"). Personalized recommendations learn from your location history and preferences.

## Key Features

- Immersive View for 3D destination previews
- Natural language search ("good spot for a first date")
- AI traffic and incident prediction
- Live View AR navigation
- Personalized restaurant and attraction recommendations
- EV charging station planning

## Pricing

- **Free**: Fully free for consumers
- **Google Maps Platform**: Pay-per-use API for developers

## Best For

Everyone — travelers planning trips, locals discovering new spots, commuters navigating traffic, and developers building location-based applications.
