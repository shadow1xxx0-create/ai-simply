Loudme is an AI music platform that generates original songs, extends existing tracks, and creates stems from text descriptions. Unlike basic generators, it gives creators more control — you can generate just the beat, just the melody, or a full track, then mix and match. Built for music producers and content creators who want AI as a creative tool, not just a vending machine.

## Key Features

- Text-to-music generation with style control
- Song extension — extend any track seamlessly
- Stem generation (drums, bass, melody separately)
- Remix and variation generation
- Royalty-free commercial license
- API for developers

## Pricing

- **Free**: 10 generations/month
- **Creator ($9.99/month)**: 100 generations, commercial use
- **Pro ($24.99/month)**: Unlimited, stems, priority

## Best For

Music producers, content creators, and developers who want more granular control over AI music generation — beyond basic track generation into stems, extensions, and creative remixing.
