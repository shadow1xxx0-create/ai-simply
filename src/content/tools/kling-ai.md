Kling AI is developed by Kuaishou (China's second-largest short video platform) and produces remarkably realistic video with consistent motion and physics. It competes directly with Sora and Runway, with strong performance in human motion, facial expressions, and complex action sequences. Available globally via the web interface.

## Key Features

- High-quality text-to-video (up to 3 minutes)
- Realistic human motion and expressions
- Image-to-video with motion control
- Camera movement controls
- Lip sync capability
- Multi-shot video generation

## Pricing

- **Free**: 66 credits/day (~10 standard generations)
- **Standard ($9.99/month)**: 660 credits/month
- **Pro ($19.99/month)**: 3,000 credits/month

## Best For

Video creators and marketers who want high-quality AI video at competitive prices, especially for content featuring realistic human motion and facial expressions.
