Headspace is one of the world's leading meditation apps, now enhanced with AI personalization. Ebb, Headspace's AI guide, provides personalized session recommendations based on your goals, schedule, and how you're feeling. With 500+ guided meditations, sleep sounds, and focus music, it covers the full spectrum of mindfulness practice.

## Key Features

- 500+ guided meditation sessions
- Ebb AI — personalized daily recommendations
- Sleepcasts and sleep sounds for better rest
- Soundscapes for focus and work
- Breathing exercises and mini-meditations
- Progress tracking and streaks

## Pricing

- **Free**: Basic meditations, limited access
- **Headspace Plus ($12.99/month)**: Full library, AI personalization
- **Family Plan ($19.99/month)**: Up to 6 members

## Best For

Beginners starting a meditation practice, professionals managing stress and burnout, and anyone wanting AI-guided mindfulness support for sleep, anxiety, and focus.
