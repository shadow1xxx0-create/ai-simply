HeyGen makes it easy to create professional videos with AI avatars — no camera, studio, or actors needed. You type a script, choose an avatar (or create one from your own face), and HeyGen generates a realistic talking-head video. The Video Translation feature can translate existing videos into 175+ languages while syncing lip movements.

## Key Features

- 100+ customizable AI avatars
- Custom avatar from your own video
- Instant video translation with lip sync (175+ languages)
- Studio-quality voice cloning
- Teleprompter recording mode
- API for video automation

## Pricing

- **Free**: 3 videos/month (1 min max)
- **Creator ($24/month)**: 15 credits/month
- **Business ($72/month)**: 30 credits, custom avatar
- **Enterprise**: Unlimited, custom workflows

## Best For

Marketing teams, L&D professionals, and content creators who need to produce multilingual video content at scale without a video production team.
