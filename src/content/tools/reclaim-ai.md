Reclaim AI automatically defends time on your Google Calendar for deep work, habits, and tasks — dynamically rescheduling them when meetings get added. Unlike static time blocks, Reclaim adjusts in real time to keep your priorities protected. Tasks sync from Asana, Jira, Todoist, and Slack.

## Key Features

- Automatic time blocking for tasks and habits
- Smart rescheduling when meetings conflict
- Task sync with Asana, Jira, Todoist, Linear
- Habit protection (e.g., daily exercise, lunch)
- Scheduling links that respect focus time
- Team sync to coordinate scheduling across teammates

## Pricing

- **Free**: 3 habits, 1 task calendar, basic features
- **Starter ($10/user/month)**: Unlimited habits and tasks
- **Business ($15/user/month)**: Team features, analytics
- **Enterprise ($20/user/month)**: SSO, priority support

## Best For

Professionals who struggle to find time for deep work and personal habits amid a full calendar of meetings — especially knowledge workers using Google Calendar.
