Murf is an AI voiceover tool designed for creating studio-quality narration without a recording studio. It's popular for e-learning courses, explainer videos, corporate presentations, and marketing content. The studio interface lets you sync voiceovers with video, add background music, and control pacing.

## Key Features

- 120+ AI voices in 20+ languages
- Video + voiceover sync editor
- Voice pitch, speed, and emphasis controls
- Background music library
- Team collaboration features
- Pronunciation editor

## Pricing

- **Free**: 10 minutes of voice generation
- **Basic ($29/month)**: 2 hours/month, commercial use
- **Pro ($39/month)**: 4 hours/month, voice cloning
- **Enterprise**: Custom usage, API

## Best For

L&D professionals, e-learning content creators, marketers, and small businesses who need professional voiceovers without hiring voice actors.
