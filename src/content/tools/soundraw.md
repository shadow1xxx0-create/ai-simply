Soundraw generates royalty-free background music customized for your content. Unlike Suno and Udio which create complete songs, Soundraw focuses on instrumental background tracks for videos, podcasts, and content — letting you control tempo, mood, length, and instruments to match your project.

## Key Features

- Royalty-free music for commercial use
- Customize length, tempo, and mood
- Select instruments and energy level
- Download in WAV or MP3
- Stem download for individual instruments
- Sync music to video automatically
