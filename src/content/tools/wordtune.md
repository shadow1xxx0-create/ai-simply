Wordtune is an AI writing tool that helps you say exactly what you mean — better. Highlight any sentence and it offers rewrites in different tones (casual, formal, witty), shortens wordy passages, expands brief notes into full paragraphs, and fixes awkward phrasing. Available as a Chrome extension that works everywhere you write online.

## Key Features

- One-click sentence rewriting with tone options
- Shorten and expand text on demand
- Spices — add statistics, examples, and counterarguments
- Works in Gmail, Docs, LinkedIn, and 1000s of sites
- AI summarization for long articles
- Multilingual support for non-native speakers

## Pricing

- **Free**: 10 rewrites/day
- **Plus ($13.99/month)**: Unlimited rewrites, Spices
- **Unlimited ($19.99/month)**: All features, priority support

## Best For

Writers, professionals, and non-native English speakers who want real-time AI suggestions to improve clarity, tone, and impact — without replacing their voice.
