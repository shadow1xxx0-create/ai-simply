Clarifai is an enterprise AI platform specializing in computer vision, NLP, and multi-modal AI. It offers pre-built models for face detection, content moderation, product recognition, and medical imaging — as well as tools for training custom vision models on your own data without deep ML expertise.

## Key Features

- Pre-trained models for 11,000+ concepts
- Custom model training with your data
- Content moderation AI
- Face detection and recognition
- Product visual search
- Workflow builder for multi-step AI pipelines

## Pricing

- **Free**: 1,000 operations/month
- **Essential ($30/month)**: 10,000 operations
- **Professional ($300/month)**: 100,000 operations, custom models
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing, SLA

## Best For

Developers, enterprises, and researchers who need reliable image and video recognition APIs — from content moderation and product search to medical imaging and security.
