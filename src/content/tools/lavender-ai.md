Lavender is an AI email coach built for sales reps. It scores your email as you write and tells you exactly what to fix to increase the chance of a reply. Connects to your CRM and LinkedIn to pull personalization data automatically.

## Key Features
- Real-time email scoring (0–100)
- AI suggestions for subject lines and openers
- LinkedIn and CRM data integration
- Mobile optimization checker
- Team analytics on email performance
