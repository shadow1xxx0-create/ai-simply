Linear is the issue tracking tool of choice for modern engineering teams, known for its exceptional speed and clean UI. Linear AI automatically triages issues, generates summaries of projects and cycles, suggests issue titles, and can create issues from Slack messages. Loved by the engineering teams at OpenAI, Vercel, Loom, and thousands of startups.

## Key Features

- AI issue triage and prioritization
- Project and cycle summaries via AI
- Automatic issue creation from Slack
- Roadmap planning with AI suggestions
- Sub-issue and dependency management
- GitHub, GitLab, Jira, and Figma integrations

## Pricing

- **Free**: Up to 250 issues, core features
- **Standard ($8/user/month)**: Unlimited issues, analytics
- **Plus ($14/user/month)**: Unlimited teams, advanced features
- **Enterprise**: SSO, custom contracts

## Best For

Engineering teams and product teams at startups and scale-ups who want a fast, focused issue tracker with AI to reduce the friction of project management.
