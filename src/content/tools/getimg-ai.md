Getimg.ai is a comprehensive AI image platform offering text-to-image generation, image editing, outpainting, inpainting, and custom AI model training in one place. With 60+ AI models available and a generous free tier, it's one of the most feature-rich AI image platforms accessible to beginners and pros alike.

## Key Features

- 60+ AI models including SDXL, Flux, and custom models
- Text-to-image, inpainting, and outpainting
- AI canvas for extended image editing
- ControlNet for pose and depth control
- Train custom AI models on your images
- API for developers

## Pricing

- **Free**: 100 images/month
- **Basic ($12/month)**: 3,000 images/month
- **Starter ($29/month)**: 12,000 images/month

## Best For

Creators and developers who want an all-in-one AI image platform with access to dozens of models, advanced editing tools, and the ability to train custom models without managing their own infrastructure.
