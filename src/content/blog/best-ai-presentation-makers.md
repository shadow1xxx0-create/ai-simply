## 1. Gamma — Best for Speed

**Pricing:** Free (400 credits) · Plus $8/mo · Pro $15/mo

Type a topic, choose a style, and Gamma generates a complete presentation in under 30 seconds. The AI writes the content, picks layouts, adds relevant icons, and structures the narrative. It's the fastest way to go from idea to shareable deck.

Gamma decks are shared as web links by default — no downloads needed. This makes them interactive: you can embed videos, live websites, polls, and GIFs directly in slides. Analytics show who viewed your deck and for how long.

The AI chat interface lets you refine after generation — "make this slide shorter," "add a comparison table," "change the tone to more formal." It iterates naturally.

**Limitation:** Less control over precise pixel-level design. If your brand has strict guidelines, Beautiful.ai gives more consistency.

**Best for:** Founders pitching investors, marketers making quick decks, anyone who needs something good fast.

## 2. Beautiful.ai — Best for Teams

**Pricing:** Pro $12/mo · Team $40/user/mo

Beautiful.ai uses "smart slides" — templates that automatically reflow and resize when you add or remove content. Add a fourth bullet point and the layout adjusts. Add a team member photo and the grid realigns. It removes the fiddling that makes PowerPoint tedious.

The Team plan adds a shared template library, brand kit (colors, fonts, logos applied across all slides), and viewer analytics. Managers can set brand-locked templates that employees can populate but not redesign — useful for keeping sales decks consistent across a large team.

**Limitation:** No free tier after the trial. More expensive per seat than Gamma.

**Best for:** Sales teams, marketing teams, and businesses that need consistent branded presentations at scale.
