## 1. Jasper — Best for Marketing Teams

**Pricing:** Creator $49/mo · Pro $69/mo · Business custom

Jasper is the enterprise-grade AI writing platform. Its Brand Voice feature trains on your company's tone, style, and terminology — then every piece of AI content stays on-brand automatically. You can upload brand guidelines, sample content, and product information to a knowledge base Jasper draws from.

Jasper Campaigns lets you generate an entire marketing campaign from a brief — blog post, social captions, email, ad copy — all consistent in voice and message. The Surfer SEO integration scores your content for keyword optimization as you write.

**Limitation:** Expensive. The $49/mo Creator plan is for solo users — teams need the $69/mo+ Pro plan. If you're not producing high volumes of content, cheaper tools do the job.

**Best for:** Marketing departments, agencies, content teams who need volume and brand consistency.

## 2. Rytr — Best Budget Option

**Pricing:** Free (10,000 chars/mo) · Unlimited $9/mo

Rytr is the most affordable AI writing assistant that's still genuinely useful. At $9/mo for unlimited generation, it undercuts every competitor significantly. It covers 40+ use cases (blog sections, email subject lines, product descriptions, social posts) across 30+ languages.

Quality is solid for short-form content. It won't match Jasper's brand sophistication, but for a solo creator, freelancer, or small business owner who needs writing help without a big budget, it's excellent value.

**Best for:** Individuals, freelancers, small businesses on a tight budget.

→ [See Rytr details and pricing](/tools/rytr/)

---

## 3. Copy.ai — Best for Sales Copy

**Pricing:** Free (2,000 words/mo) · Starter $49/mo · Advanced $249/mo

Copy.ai has evolved from a simple copy generator into a sales and marketing automation platform. Its "Workflows" feature lets you build automated pipelines — pull a lead's LinkedIn profile, generate a personalized cold email, then push it to your CRM. For sales teams, it's closer to a workflow automation tool than a writing assistant.

The 90+ templates cover every sales and marketing format: cold emails, LinkedIn messages, product launch copy, landing pages, ad headlines. The free tier is generous enough to evaluate properly.

**Best for:** Sales teams, growth marketers, anyone who needs automated personalized outreach.

→ [See Copy.ai details and pricing](/tools/copy-ai/)

---

## 4. Writesonic — Best for SEO Articles

**Pricing:** Free (10,000 words/mo) · Individual $20/mo · Teams $30/user/mo

Writesonic focuses on long-form SEO content. Its "AI Article Writer 6.0" generates complete blog posts with real-time web research, fact-checking, and internal linking suggestions. The Surfer SEO integration ensures the output is optimized for your target keyword before you publish.

For content marketers running a blog SEO strategy, Writesonic's end-to-end article workflow — keyword to published, fact-checked post — saves significant time.

**Best for:** Bloggers, SEO content marketers, teams that produce high-volume articles.

→ [See Writesonic details and pricing](/tools/writesonic/)
